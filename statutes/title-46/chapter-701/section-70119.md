# § 70119. Civil penalty

- **(a)** Any person that violates this chapter or any regulation under this chapter shall be liable to the United States for a civil penalty of not more than $25,000 for each day during which the violation continues.
- **(b)** The maximum amount of a civil penalty for a violation under this section shall not exceed $50,000.
