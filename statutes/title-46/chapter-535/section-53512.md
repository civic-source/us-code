# § 53512. FIFO and LIFO withdrawals

- **(a)** Except as provided in subsection (b), an amount withdrawn from an account under this chapter shall be treated as withdrawn on a first-in-first-out basis.
- **(b)** An amount withdrawn from an account under this chapter shall be treated as withdrawn on a last-in-first-out basis if it is—
  - **(1)** a nonqualified withdrawal for research, development, and design expenses incident to new and advanced vessel design, machinery, and equipment; or
  - **(2)** an amount treated as a nonqualified withdrawal under section 53510(d) of this title .
