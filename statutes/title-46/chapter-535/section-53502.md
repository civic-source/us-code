# § 53502. Regulations

- **(a)** Except as provided in subsection (b), the Secretary shall prescribe regulations to carry out this chapter.
- **(b)** The Secretary and the Secretary of the Treasury shall prescribe joint regulations for the determination of tax liability under this chapter.
