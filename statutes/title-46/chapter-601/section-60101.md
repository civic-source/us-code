# § 60101. Boarding arriving vessels before inspection

- **(a)** The Secretary of Homeland Security shall prescribe and enforce regulations on the boarding of a vessel arriving at a port of the United States before the vessel has been inspected and secured.
- **(b)** A person violating a regulation prescribed under this section shall be fined under title 18, imprisoned for not more than 6 months, or both.
- **(c)** This section shall be construed as supplementary to section 2279 of title 18 .
