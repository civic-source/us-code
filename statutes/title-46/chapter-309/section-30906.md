# § 30906. Venue

- **(a)** A civil action under this chapter shall be brought in the district court of the United States for the district in which—
  - **(1)** any plaintiff resides or has its principal place of business; or
  - **(2)** the vessel or cargo is found.
- **(b)** On a motion by a party, the court may transfer the action to any other district court of the United States.
