# § 7307. Able seamen—unlimited

The required service for the endorsement of able seaman—unlimited, qualified for unlimited service on a vessel on any waters, is at least 3 years’ 1 1 See Temporary Reduction of Lengths of Certain Periods of Service note below. service on deck on board vessels operating at sea or on the Great Lakes.
