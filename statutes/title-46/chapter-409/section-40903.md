# § 40903. Suspension or revocation of license

- **(a)** The Federal Maritime Commission, after notice and opportunity for a hearing, shall suspend or revoke an ocean transportation intermediary’s license if the Commission finds that the ocean transportation intermediary—
  - **(1)** is not qualified to provide intermediary services; or
  - **(2)** willfully failed to comply with a provision of this part or with an order or regulation of the Commission.
- **(b)** The Commission may revoke an ocean transportation intermediary’s license for failure to maintain a bond, proof of insurance, or other surety as required by section 40902(a) of this title .
