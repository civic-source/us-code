# § 103. Boundary Line

In this title, the term “Boundary Line” means a line established under section 2(b) of the Act of February 19, 1895 ( 33 U.S.C. 151 ). 1 1 So in original. Probably should be “( 33 U.S.C. 151(b) ).”.
