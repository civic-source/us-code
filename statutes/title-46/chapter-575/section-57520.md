# § 57520. Vessel maintenance

- **(a)** A charter under this chapter shall require the charterer, at the charterer’s expense, to—
  - **(1)** keep the chartered vessel in good repair and efficient operating condition; and
  - **(2)** make any repairs required by the Secretary of Transportation.
- **(b)** The charter shall provide that the Secretary has the right to inspect the vessel at any time to ascertain its condition.
