# § 14511. Application

This subchapter applies to a vessel described in section 14501 of this title if—

  - **(1)** the owner requests; or
  - **(2)** the vessel is—
    - **(A)** self-propelled;
    - **(B)** at least 24 meters (79 feet) overall in length; and
    - **(C)** not operated only for pleasure.
