# § 30705. Seaworthiness

- **(a)** A carrier may not insert in a bill of lading or shipping document a provision lessening or avoiding its obligation to exercise due diligence to—
  - **(1)** make the vessel seaworthy; and
  - **(2)** properly man, equip, and supply the vessel.
- **(b)** A provision described in subsection (a) is void.
