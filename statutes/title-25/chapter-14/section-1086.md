# § 1086. Advances or expenditures from tribal funds

The Secretary is authorized to advance or expend, as provided in section 1081 of this title , the Hoh tribal funds now on deposit, or hereafter placed on deposit, in the Treasury of the United States under the following symbols and titles: 14X7235 Proceeds of Labor, Hoh Indians, Washington; 14X7735 Interest and Accruals on Interest, Proceeds of Labor, Hoh Indians, Washington.
