# § 1775e. Ratification of Town Agreement

- **(a)** Notwithstanding any other provision of law, the consent of the United States is hereby given to the Town Agreement and the Town Agreement shall be in full force and effect.
- **(b)** The Secretary shall approve any subsequent amendments made to the Town Agreement after October 19, 1994 , that are—
  - **(1)** mutually agreed on by the parties to the Town Agreement; and
  - **(2)** consistent with applicable law.
