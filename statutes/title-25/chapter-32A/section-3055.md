# § 3055. Forest products for traditional and cultural purposes

- **(a)** Notwithstanding section 472a of title 16 , the Secretary may provide free of charge to Indian tribes any trees, portions of trees, or forest products from National Forest System land for traditional and cultural purposes.
- **(b)** Trees, portions of trees, or forest products provided under subsection (a) may not be used for commercial purposes.
