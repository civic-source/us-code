# § 5632. Definitions

In this subchapter:

  - **(1)** The term “BIA” means the Bureau of Indian Affairs.
  - **(2)** The term “Department” means the Department of the Interior.
  - **(3)** The term “Secretary” means the Secretary of the Interior.
  - **(4)** The term “Under Secretary” means the Under Secretary for Indian Affairs established under section 5633(a) of this title .
