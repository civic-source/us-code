# § 3745. Authorization of appropriations

- **(a)** There are authorized to be appropriated such sums as may be necessary to carry out the purposes of this chapter.
- **(b)** The activities required under subchapter II of this chapter may only be funded from appropriations made pursuant to this chapter. To the greatest extent possible, such activities shall be coordinated with activities funded from other sources.
