# § 316. Rights of several roads through canyons

The provisions of section 935 1 1 See References in Text note below. of title 43 relating to the rights of several railroads through any canyon, pass, or defile are extended and made applicable to rights of way granted under sections 312 to 318 of this title and to railroad companies obtaining such rights of way.
