# § 5802. Definitions

In this chapter:

  - **(1)** The term “business incubator” means an organization that—
    - **(A)** provides physical workspace and facilities resources to startups and established businesses; and
    - **(B)** is designed to accelerate the growth and success of businesses through a variety of business support resources and services, including—
      - **(i)** access to capital, business education, and counseling;
      - **(ii)** networking opportunities;
      - **(iii)** mentorship opportunities; and
      - **(iv)** other services intended to aid in developing a business.
  - **(2)** The term “eligible applicant” means an applicant eligible to apply for a grant under section 5803(b) of this title .
  - **(3)** The term “Indian tribe” has the meaning given the term in section 5304 of this title .
  - **(4)** The term “institution of higher education” has the meaning given the term in section 1001 of title 20 .
  - **(5)** The terms “Native American” and “Native” have the meaning given the term “Indian” in section 5304 of this title .
  - **(6)** The term “Native business” means a business concern that is at least 51-percent owned and controlled by 1 or more Native Americans.
  - **(7)** The term “Native entrepreneur” means an entrepreneur who is a Native American.
  - **(8)** The term “program” means the program established under section 5803(a) of this title .
  - **(9)** The term “reservation” has the meaning given the term in section 1452 of this title .
  - **(10)** The term “Secretary” means the Secretary of the Interior.
  - **(11)** The term “tribal college or university” has the meaning given the term “Tribal College or University” in section 1059c(b) of title 20 .
