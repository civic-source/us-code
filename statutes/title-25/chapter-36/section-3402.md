# § 3402. Definitions

For the purposes of this chapter, the following definitions apply:

  - **(1)** The term “federal 1 1 So in original. Probably should be capitalized. agency” has the same meaning given the term “agency” in section 551(1) of title 5 .
  - **(2)** The terms “Indian tribe” and “tribe” shall have the meaning given the term “Indian tribe” in section 450b(e) of this title .
  - **(3)** The term “Indian” shall have the meaning given such term in section 450b(d) of this title .
  - **(4)** Except where otherwise provided, the term “Secretary” means the Secretary of the Interior.
