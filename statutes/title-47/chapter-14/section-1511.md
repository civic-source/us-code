# § 1511. Rules of construction

- **(a)** Each range of frequencies described in this chapter shall be construed to be inclusive of the upper and lower frequencies in the range.
- **(b)** Nothing in this chapter shall be construed to affect any requirement under section 156 of the National Telecommunications and Information Administration Organization Act ( 47 U.S.C. 921 note), as added by section 1062(a) of the National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2000.
