# § 1105. Annual audit

- **(a)** The Comptroller General of the United States shall conduct on an annual basis an audit of—
  - **(1)** the administration of the provisions of this chapter; and
  - **(2)** the financial position of each applicant who receives a loan guarantee under this chapter, including the nature, amount, and purpose of investments made by the applicant.
- **(b)** The Comptroller General shall submit to the Committee on Banking, Housing, and Urban Affairs of the Senate and the Committee on Banking and Financial Services of the House of Representatives a report on each audit conducted under subsection (a) of this section.
