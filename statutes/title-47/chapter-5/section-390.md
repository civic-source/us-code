# § 390. Declaration of purpose

  - **(1)** extend delivery of public telecommunications services to as many citizens of the United States as possible by the most efficient and economical means, including the use of broadcast and nonbroadcast technologies;
  - **(2)** increase public telecommunications services and facilities available to, operated by, and owned by minorities and women; and
  - **(3)** strengthen the capability of existing public television and radio stations to provide public telecommunications services to the public.
