# § 782. Selection of trustee

- **(a)**
  - **(1)** Notwithstanding any other provision of this title, the conservator or receiver who files the petition shall be the trustee under this chapter, unless the Board designates an alternative trustee.
  - **(2)** The Board may designate a successor trustee if required.
- **(b)** Whenever the Board appoints or designates a trustee, chapter 3 and sections 704 and 705 of this title shall apply to the Board in the same way and to the same extent that they apply to a United States trustee.
