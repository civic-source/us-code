# § 1541. Establishment of Advisory Commission

- **(a)** There is established a commission to be known as the “Advisory Commission on Drug-Free Communities”.
- **(b)** The Advisory Commission shall advise, consult with, and make recommendations to the Director concerning matters related to the activities carried out under the Program.
