# § 4210. Sources of payments to declarants

Notwithstanding any other law, an award under this chapter 1 1 See References in Text note below. may be paid to a declarant, or to an individual providing information, from the amounts recovered through civil actions based in whole or in part on the information provided in a valid declaration under this chapter. 1
