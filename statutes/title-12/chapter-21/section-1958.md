# § 1958. Compliance

The Secretary shall have the responsibility to assure compliance with the requirements of this chapter and sections 1730d 1 1 See References in Text note below. and 1829b of this title and may delegate such responsibility to the appropriate bank supervisory agency, or other supervisory agency.
