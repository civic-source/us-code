# § 4511. Establishment of the Federal Housing Finance Agency

- **(a)** There is established the Federal Housing Finance Agency, which shall be an independent agency of the Federal Government.
- **(b)**
  - **(1)** Each regulated entity shall, to the extent provided in this chapter, be subject to the supervision and regulation of the Agency.
  - **(2)** The Director shall have general regulatory authority over each regulated entity and the Office of Finance, and shall exercise such general regulatory authority, including such duties and authorities set forth under section 4513 of this title , to ensure that the purposes of this Act, the authorizing statutes, and any other applicable law are carried out.
- **(c)** The authority of the Director to take actions under subchapters II and III shall not in any way limit the general supervisory and regulatory authority granted to the Director under subsection (b).
