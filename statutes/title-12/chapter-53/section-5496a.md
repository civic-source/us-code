# § 5496a. Annual audits

- **(a)** The Bureau shall order an annual independent audit of the operations and budget of the Bureau.
- **(b)** The Comptroller General of the United States shall conduct an annual audit of the Bureau’s financial statements in accordance with generally accepted government accounting standards.
