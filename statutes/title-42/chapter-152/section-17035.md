# § 17035. University based research and development grant program

- **(a)** The Secretary shall establish a competitive grant program, in a geographically diverse manner, for projects submitted for consideration by institutions of higher education to conduct research and development of renewable energy technologies. Each grant made shall not exceed $2,000,000.
- **(b)** Priority shall be given to institutions of higher education with—
  - **(1)** established programs of research in renewable energy;
  - **(2)** locations that are low income or outside of an urbanized area;
  - **(3)** a joint venture with an Indian tribe; and
  - **(4)** proximity to trees dying of disease or insect infestation as a source of woody biomass.
- **(c)** There are authorized to be appropriated to the Secretary $25,000,000 for carrying out this section.
- **(d)** In this section:
  - **(1)** The term “Indian tribe” has the meaning as defined in section 15823(c) of this title .
  - **(2)** The term “renewable energy” has the meaning as defined in section 16181 of this title .
  - **(3)** The term “urbanized area” has the meaning as defined by the U.S. Bureau of the Census.
