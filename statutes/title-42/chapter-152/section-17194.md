# § 17194. Enhanced geothermal systems research and development

- **(a)** The Secretary shall support a program of research, development, demonstration, and commercial application for enhanced geothermal systems, including the programs described in subsection (b).
- **(b)**
  - **(1)** The Secretary shall support a program of research, development, demonstration, and commercial application of the technologies and knowledge necessary for enhanced geothermal systems to advance to a state of commercial readiness, including advances in—
    - **(A)** reservoir stimulation;
    - **(B)** reservoir characterization, monitoring, and modeling;
    - **(C)** stress mapping;
    - **(D)** tracer development;
    - **(E)** three-dimensional tomography; and
    - **(F)** understanding seismic effects of reservoir engineering and stimulation.
  - **(2)**
    - **(A)** In collaboration with industry partners, the Secretary shall support a program of research, development, and demonstration of enhanced geothermal systems reservoir stimulation technologies and techniques. A minimum of 4 sites shall be selected in locations that show particular promise for enhanced geothermal systems development. Each site shall—
      - **(i)** represent a different class of subsurface geologic environments; and
      - **(ii)** take advantage of an existing site where subsurface characterization has been conducted or existing drill holes can be utilized, if possible.
    - **(B)** The Desert Peak, Nevada, site, where a Department of Energy and industry cooperative enhanced geothermal systems project is already underway, may be considered for inclusion among the sites selected under subparagraph (A).
