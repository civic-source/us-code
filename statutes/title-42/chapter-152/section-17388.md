# § 17388. Advisory committee

- **(a)** Not later than 180 days after December 27, 2020 , the Secretary shall designate an existing advisory committee to advise the Secretary on the authorization of research, development, and demonstration projects under sections 17384 and 17384a of this title.
- **(b)** The Secretary shall annually solicit from the advisory committee—
  - **(1)** comments to identify grid modernization technology needs;
  - **(2)** an assessment of the progress of the research activities on grid modernization; and
  - **(3)** assistance in annually updating grid modernization technology roadmaps.
