# § 7142d. Definitions

For purposes of sections 7142a to 7142d of this title:

  - **(1)** The term “Museum” means the museum operated by the Secretary of Energy and located at 300 South Tulane Avenue in Oak Ridge, Tennessee.
  - **(2)** The term “Secretary” means the Secretary of Energy or a designated representative of the Secretary.
