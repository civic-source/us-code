# § 18302. Definitions

In this chapter:

  - **(1)** The term “Administrator” means the Administrator of the National Aeronautics and Space Administration.
  - **(2)** The term “appropriate committees of Congress” means—
    - **(A)** the Committee on Commerce, Science, and Transportation of the Senate; and
    - **(B)** the Committee on Science 1 1 So in original. Probably should be followed by “and Technology”. of the House of Representatives.
  - **(3)** The term “cis-lunar space” means the region of space from the Earth out to and including the region around the surface of the Moon.
  - **(4)** The term “deep space” means the region of space beyond cis-lunar space.
  - **(5)** The term “ISS” means the International Space Station.
  - **(6)** The term “NASA” means the National Aeronautics and Space Administration.
  - **(7)** The term “near-Earth space” means the region of space that includes low-Earth orbit and extends out to and includes geo-synchronous orbit.
  - **(8)** The term “NOAA” means the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration.
  - **(9)** The term “OSTP” means the Office of Science and Technology Policy.
  - **(10)** The term “Space Launch System” means the follow-on government-owned civil launch system developed, managed, and operated by NASA to serve as a key component to expand human presence beyond low-Earth orbit.
