# § 3057n. Authorization of appropriations

There are authorized to be appropriated to carry out this subchapter—

  - **(1)** for parts A and B of this subchapter, $31,934,018 for fiscal year 2017, $32,601,843 for fiscal year 2018, and $33,269,670 for fiscal year 2019; and
  - **(2)** for part C of this subchapter, $7,718,566 for fiscal year 2017, $7,879,982 for fiscal year 2018, and $8,041,398 for fiscal year 2019.
