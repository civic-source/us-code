# § 15029. Authorization of appropriations

- **(a)** Except as described in subsection (b) of this section, there are authorized to be appropriated for allotments under section 15022 of this title $76,000,000 for fiscal year 2001 and such sums as may be necessary for each of fiscal years 2002 through 2007.
- **(b)**
  - **(1)** For any fiscal year for which the amount appropriated under subsection (a) of this section is less than $76,000,000, the Secretary shall reserve funds in accordance with section 15083(c) of this title to provide technical assistance to entities funded under this part.
  - **(2)** For any fiscal year for which the amount appropriated under subsection (a) of this section is not less than $76,000,000, the Secretary shall reserve not less than $300,000 and not more than 1 percent of the amount appropriated under subsection (a) of this section to provide technical assistance to entities funded under this part.
