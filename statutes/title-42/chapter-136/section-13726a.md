# § 13726a. Definitions

In sections 13726 to 13726c of this title:

  - **(1)** The term “crime of violence” has the same meaning as in section 924(c)(3) of title 18 .
  - **(2)** The term “private prisoner transport company” means any entity, other than the United States, a State, or an inferior political subdivision of a State, which engages in the business of the transporting for compensation, individuals committed to the custody of any State or of an inferior political subdivision of a State, or any attempt thereof.
  - **(3)** The term “violent prisoner” means any individual in the custody of a State or an inferior political subdivision of a State who has previously been convicted of or is currently charged with a crime of violence or any similar statute of a State or the inferior political subdivisions of a State, or any attempt thereof.
