# § 5064. Review of project renewals

  - **(1)** review such statement and take it into account in determining whether to renew such contract or grant; and
  - **(2)** submit to such executive authority a written statement of reasons regarding the Director’s determination with respect to such renewal and specifically with respect to any objection so submitted.
