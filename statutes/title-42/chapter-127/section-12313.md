# § 12313. Federal agency consultations

- **(a)** The Commissioner shall consult and cooperate with the heads of all appropriate Federal agencies or departments administering programs or services that are substantially related to the purposes of this chapter.
- **(b)** To the extent practicable, the Commissioner shall facilitate cooperation through the entering into of interagency agreements.
