# § 12522. Definitions

In this part:

  - **(1)** The term “State” means each of the several States, the District of Columbia, and the Commonwealth of Puerto Rico.
  - **(2)** The term “State educational agency” means—
    - **(A)** a State educational agency (as defined in section 12511 of this title ) of a State; or
    - **(B)** for a State in which a State educational agency described in subparagraph (A) has designated a statewide entity under section 12523(e) 1 1 See References in Text note below. of this title, that designated statewide entity.
