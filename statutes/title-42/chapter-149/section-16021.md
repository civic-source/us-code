# § 16021. Project establishment

- **(a)** The Secretary shall establish a project to be known as the “Next Generation Nuclear Plant Project” (referred to in this part as the “Project”).
- **(b)** The Project shall consist of the research, development, design, construction, and operation of a prototype plant, including a nuclear reactor that—
  - **(1)** is based on research and development activities supported by the Generation IV Nuclear Energy Systems Initiative under section 16272(c) 1 1 See References in Text note below. of this title; and
  - **(2)** shall be used—
    - **(A)** to generate electricity;
    - **(B)** to produce hydrogen; or
    - **(C)** both to generate electricity and to produce hydrogen.
