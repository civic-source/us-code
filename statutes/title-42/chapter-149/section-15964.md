# § 15964. Clean coal centers of excellence

- **(a)** As part of the clean coal power initiative, the Secretary shall award competitive, merit-based grants to institutions of higher education for the establishment of centers of excellence for energy systems of the future.
- **(b)** The Secretary shall award grants under this section to institutions of higher education that show the greatest potential for advancing new clean coal technologies.
