# § 13215. Agency incentives program

- **(a)** To encourage and promote use of alternative fueled vehicles in Federal agencies, the Administrator of General Services may offer a reduction in fees charged to agencies for the lease of alternative fueled vehicles below those fees charged for the lease of comparable conventionally fueled motor vehicles.
- **(b)** This section shall cease to be effective 3 years after October 24, 1992 .
