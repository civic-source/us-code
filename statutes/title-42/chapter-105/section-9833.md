# § 9833. Financial assistance for Head Start programs

  - **(1)** will provide such comprehensive health, education, parental involvement, nutritional, social, and other services as will enable the children to attain their full potential and attain school readiness; and
  - **(2)** will provide for direct participation of the parents of such children in the development, conduct, and overall program direction at the local level.
