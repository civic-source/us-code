# § 18642. Advanced scientific computing research

- **(a)**
- **(b)** The Director shall support research in high-performance computing and networking relevant to energy applications, including modeling, simulation, and advanced data analytics for basic and applied energy research programs carried out by the Secretary.
- **(c)** The Director shall carry out activities to develop, test, and support—
  - **(1)** mathematics, models, and algorithms for complex systems and programming environments; and
  - **(2)** tools, languages, and operating systems for high-end computing systems (as defined in section 5541 of title 15 ).
