# § 10370. Effect

- **(a)** Nothing in this chapter supersedes or limits any existing authority provided, or responsibility conferred, by any provision of law.
- **(b)**
  - **(1)** Nothing in this chapter preempts or affects any—
    - **(A)** State water law; or
    - **(B)** interstate compact governing water.
  - **(2)** The Secretary shall comply with applicable State water laws in carrying out this chapter.
