# § 18701. Definitions

In this division:

  - **(1)** The term “Department” means the Department of Energy.
  - **(2)** The term “Indian Tribe” has the meaning given the term in section 5304 of title 25 .
  - **(3)** The term “Secretary” means the Secretary of Energy.
