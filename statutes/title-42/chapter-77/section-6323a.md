# § 6323a. Matching State contributions

For the base State Energy Conservation Program (part D of the Energy Policy and Conservation Act, sections 361 through 366 [ 42 U.S.C. 6321–632 6]), each State will hereafter match in cash or in kind not less than 20 percent of the Federal contribution.
