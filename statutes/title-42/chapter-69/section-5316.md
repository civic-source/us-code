# § 5316. Transition provisions

- **(a)** Except with respect to projects and programs for which funds have been previously committed, no new grants or loans shall be made after January 1, 1975 , under (1) title I of the Demonstration Cities and Metropolitan Development Act of 1966 [ 42 U.S.C. 3301 et seq.], (2) title I of the Housing Act of 1949 [ 42 U.S.C. 1450 et seq.] (3) section 702 or section 703 of the Housing and Urban Development Act of 1965 [ 42 U.S.C. 3102 or 3103], (4) title II of the Housing Amendments of 1955 [ 42 U.S.C. 1491 et seq.], or (5) title VII of the Housing Act of 1961 [ 42 U.S.C. 1500 et seq.].
- **(b)** In the case of funds available for any fiscal year, the Secretary shall not consider any statement under section 5304(a) of this title , unless such statement is submitted on or prior to such date as the Secretary shall establish as the final date for submission of statements for that year.
