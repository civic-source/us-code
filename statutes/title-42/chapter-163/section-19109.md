# § 19109. Translation Accelerator

- **(a)** The Director shall establish Translation Accelerators to further the research, development, and commercialization of innovation in the key technology focus areas.
- **(b)**
  - **(1)** Each Translation Accelerator shall be comprised of a partnership including 2 or more of the following entities:
    - **(A)** An institution of higher education.
    - **(B)** A for-profit company.
    - **(C)** A nonprofit organization.
    - **(D)** A Federal agency.
    - **(E)** Another entity, if that entity is determined by the Director to be vital to the success of the program.
  - **(2)** The Director shall work to ensure that such partnerships exist at the institutional or organization level, rather than solely at the principal investigator level.
  - **(3)** Not less than 25 percent of the funding for an institute shall be provided by non-Federal entities.
  - **(4)** The Director shall endeavor to establish a balance in the number of Regional Innovation Engines and Translation Accelerators.
- **(c)** From within funds authorized for the Directorate for Technology, Innovation, and Partnerships, there are authorized to carry out the activities under this section and section 19108 of this title $6,500,000,000 for fiscal years 2023 through 2027.
