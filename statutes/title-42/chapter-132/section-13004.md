# § 13004. Authorization of appropriations

- **(a)** There are authorized to be appropriated to carry out sections 13001b and 13002 of this title, $15,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2014, 2015, 2016, 2017, and 2018.
- **(b)** There are authorized to be appropriated to carry out section 13003 of this title , $5,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2014, 2015, 2016, 2017, and 2018.
