# § 15545. No effect on other laws

- **(a)** Except as specifically provided in section 15483(b) of this title with regard to the National Voter Registration Act of 1993 ( 42 U.S.C. 1973gg et seq.), nothing in this chapter may be construed to authorize or require conduct prohibited under any of the following laws, or to supersede, restrict, or limit the application of such laws:
  - **(1)** The Voting Rights Act of 1965 ( 42 U.S.C. 1973 et seq.).
  - **(2)** The Voting Accessibility for the Elderly and Handicapped Act ( 42 U.S.C. 1973ee et seq.).
  - **(3)** The Uniformed and Overseas Citizens Absentee Voting Act ( 42 U.S.C. 1973ff et seq.).
  - **(4)** The National Voter Registration Act of 1993 ( 42 U.S.C. 1973gg et seq.).
  - **(5)** The Americans with Disabilities Act of 1990 ( 42 U.S.C. 12101 et seq.).
  - **(6)** The Rehabilitation Act of 1973 ( 29 U.S.C. 701 et seq.).
- **(b)** The approval by the Administrator or the Commission of a payment or grant application under subchapter I or subchapter II of this chapter, or any other action taken by the Commission or a State under such subchapter, shall not be considered to have any effect on requirements for preclearance under section 5 of the Voting Rights Act of 1965 ( 42 U.S.C. 1973c ) or any other requirements of such Act [ 42 U.S.C. 1973 et seq.].
