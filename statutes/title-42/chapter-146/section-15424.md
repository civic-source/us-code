# § 15424. Authorization of appropriations

- **(a)** There are authorized to be appropriated to carry out the provisions of this subpart the following amounts:
  - **(1)** For fiscal year 2003, $50,000,000.
  - **(2)** For fiscal year 2004, $25,000,000.
  - **(3)** For fiscal year 2005, $25,000,000.
- **(b)** Any amounts appropriated pursuant to the authority of subsection (a) of this section shall remain available without fiscal year limitation until expended.
