# § 14612. Definitions

In this subchapter:

  - **(1)** The term “Attorney General” means the Attorney General of the United States.
  - **(2)** The term “Compact” means the National Crime Prevention and Privacy Compact set forth in section 14616 of this title .
  - **(3)** The term “Council” means the Compact Council established under Article VI of the Compact.
  - **(4)** The term “FBI” means the Federal Bureau of Investigation.
  - **(5)** The term “Party State” means a State that has ratified the Compact.
  - **(6)** The term “State” means any State, territory, or possession of the United States, the District of Columbia, and the Commonwealth of Puerto Rico.
