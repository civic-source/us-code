# § 1786. Care for newborn children of women veterans receiving maternity care

- **(a)** The Secretary may furnish health care services described in subsection (b) to a newborn child of a woman veteran who is receiving maternity care furnished by the Department for not more than seven days after the birth of the child if the veteran delivered the child in—
  - **(1)** a facility of the Department; or
  - **(2)** another facility pursuant to a Department contract for services relating to such delivery.
- **(b)** Health care services described in this subsection are all post-delivery care services, including routine care services, that a newborn child requires.
