# § 7804. Financing of Service

  - **(1)** the acquisition of necessary furniture, furnishings, fixtures, and equipment for the establishment, maintenance, and operation of canteens, warehouses, and storage depots;
  - **(2)** stocks of merchandise and supplies for canteens and reserve stocks of same in warehouses and storage depots;
  - **(3)** salaries, wages, and expenses of all employees;
  - **(4)** administrative and operation expenses; and
  - **(5)** adequate working capital for each canteen and for the Service as a whole. Amounts appropriated under the authority contained in this chapter and all income from canteen operations become and will be administered as a revolving fund to effectuate the provisions of this chapter.
