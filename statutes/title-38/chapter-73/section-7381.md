# § 7381. Office of Research and Development

- **(a)** There is in the Veterans Health Administration an Office of Research and Development (in this section referred to as the “Office”).
- **(b)** The function of the Office is to serve veterans through a full spectrum of research (including pre-clinical, clinical, and health systems science), technology transfer, and application.
- **(c)** The head of the Office is the Chief Research and Development Officer.
- **(d)** The Office shall be organized in such manner, and its personnel shall perform such duties and have such titles, as the Secretary may prescribe.
