# § 6307. Outreach for eligible dependents

- **(a)** In carrying out this chapter, the Secretary shall ensure that the needs of eligible dependents are fully addressed.
- **(b)** The Secretary shall ensure that the availability of outreach services and assistance for eligible dependents under this chapter is made known through a variety of means, including the Internet, announcements in veterans publications, and announcements to the media.
