# § 1421b. Stranding response agreements

- **(a)** The Secretary may enter into an agreement under section 1382(c) of this title with any person to take marine mammals under section 1379(h)(1) of this title in response to a stranding.
- **(b)** An agreement authorized by subsection (a) of this section shall—
  - **(1)** specify each person who is authorized to perform activities under the agreement; and
  - **(2)** specify any terms and conditions under which a person so specified may delegate that authority to another person.
- **(c)** The Secretary shall periodically review agreements under section 1382(c) of this title that are entered into pursuant to this subchapter, for performance adequacy and effectiveness.
