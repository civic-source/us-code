# § 6851. Definitions

In this chapter:

  - **(1)** The term “Federal land” means any land managed by a Federal land management agency.
  - **(2)** The term “Federal land management agency” means—
    - **(A)** the Bureau of Reclamation;
    - **(B)** the National Park Service;
    - **(C)** the Bureau of Land Management;
    - **(D)** the United States Fish and Wildlife Service;
    - **(E)** the Forest Service; and
    - **(F)** the Corps of Engineers.
  - **(3)** The term “road or trail” means a road or trail designated by 1 or more of the Secretaries for public use.
  - **(4)** The term “Secretaries” means—
    - **(A)** the Secretary of Agriculture, acting through the Chief of the Forest Service;
    - **(B)** the Secretary of the Interior; and
    - **(C)** the Secretary of the Army, acting through the Assistant Secretary of the Army for Civil Works.
