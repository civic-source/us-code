# § 695m. Annual percentage payments of net revenues from leases of Klamath project lands on pro rata basis; limitation on payments; priority of use of net revenues

  - **(1)** to credit or pay from each revenues to the Tulelake Irrigation District the amounts already committed to such payment or credit;
  - **(2)** to pay from such revenues to the Klamath Drainage District the sum of $197,315; and
  - **(3)** to pay from such revenues to the counties the amounts prescribed by this section.
