# § 8211. Nonapplicability of Federal Advisory Committee Act

The Federal Advisory Committee Act (5 U.S.C. App.) 1 1 See References in Text note below. shall not apply to—

  - **(1)** the Board; or
  - **(2)** any Partnership.
