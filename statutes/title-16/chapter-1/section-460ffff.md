# § 460ffff. Definitions

In this subchapter:

  - **(1)** The term “management plan” means the management plan for the Scenic Area developed under section 460ffff–2(a) of this title .
  - **(2)** The term “Map” means the map entitled “Proposed Alabama Hills National Scenic Area” and dated November 7, 2018 .
  - **(3)** The term “motorized vehicle” means a motorized or mechanized vehicle and includes, when used by a utility, mechanized equipment, a helicopter, and any other aerial device necessary to maintain electrical or communications infrastructure.
  - **(4)** The term “Scenic Area” means the Alabama Hills National Scenic Area established by section 460ffff–1(a) of this title .
  - **(5)** The term “State” means the State of California.
  - **(6)** The term “Tribe” means the Lone Pine Paiute-Shoshone Tribe.
