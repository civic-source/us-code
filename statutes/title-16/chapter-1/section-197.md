# § 197. Applicability of other laws

The provisions of the Act of January 26, 1915 , entitled “An Act to establish the Rocky Mountain National Park in the State of Colorado, and for other purposes,” and Act of August 25, 1916 , entitled “An Act to establish a national–park service, and for other purposes,” 1 1 See References in Text note below. and all Acts supplementary to and amendatory of said Acts are made applicable to and extended over the lands hereby added to the park: Provided , That the provisions of the Federal Power Act [ 16 U.S.C. 791a et seq.] shall not apply to or extend over such lands.
