# § 460ooo. Definitions

For the purposes of this subchapter, the following definitions apply:

  - **(1)** The term “Conservation Area” means the Las Cienegas National Conservation Area established by section 460 ooo –3(a) of this title.
  - **(2)** The term “Acquisition Planning District” means the Sonoita Valley Acquisition Planning District established by section 460 ooo –1(a) of this title.
  - **(3)** The term “management plan” means the management plan for the Conservation Area.
  - **(4)** The term “public lands” has the meaning given the term in section 1702(e) of title 43 , except that such term shall not include interest in lands not owned by the United States.
  - **(5)** The term “Secretary” means the Secretary of the Interior.
