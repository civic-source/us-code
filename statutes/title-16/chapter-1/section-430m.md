# § 430m. Administration

The administration, development, preservation, and maintenance of the battlefield shall be exercised by the Secretary of the Interior in accordance with the Act of August 25, 1916 ( 39 Stat. 535 ; 16 U.S.C. 1 et seq.), 1 1 See References in Text note below. as amended and supplemented, and the Act of August 21, 1935 ( 49 Stat. 666 ). 1
