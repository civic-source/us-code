# § 460zzz. Definitions

In this subchapter:

  - **(1)** The term “Conservation Area” means the Dominguez-Escalante National Conservation Area established by section 460zzz–1(a)(1) of this title .
  - **(2)** The term “Council” means the Dominguez-Escalante National Conservation Area Advisory Council established under section 460zzz–6 of this title .
  - **(3)** The term “management plan” means the management plan developed under section 460zzz–5 of this title .
  - **(4)** The term “Map” means the map entitled “Dominguez-Escalante National Conservation Area” and dated September 15, 2008 .
  - **(5)** The term “Secretary” means the Secretary of the Interior.
  - **(6)** The term “State” means the State of Colorado.
  - **(7)** The term “Wilderness” means the Dominguez Canyon Wilderness Area designated by section 460zzz–2(a) of this title .
