# § 397d. Administration

The Puʻuhonua o Ho̅naunau National Historical Park shall be administered by the Secretary of the Interior subject to the provisions of the Act of August 25, 1916 ( 39 Stat. 535 ; 16 U.S.C., 1946 edition, secs. 1–4), 1 1 See References in Text note below. as amended and supplemented, and such additional authority compatible therewith as is contained in the Act of August 21, 1935 ( 49 Stat. 666 ; 16 U.S.C., 1946 edition, secs. 461–467), 1 with regard to preservation of historic sites and objects of national significance.
