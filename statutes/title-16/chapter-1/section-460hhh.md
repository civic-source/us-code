# § 460hhh. Definitions

As used in this subchapter:

  - **(1)** The term “National Forest lands” means lands included in the National Forest System (as defined in section 1609(a) of this title ).
  - **(2)** The term “Recreation Area” means the Spring Mountains National Recreation Area established by this subchapter.
  - **(3)** The term “Secretary” means the Secretary of Agriculture.
