# § 282c. Authorization of appropriations

There are hereby authorized to be appropriated such sums, but not more than $13,575,000 for the acquisition of lands and interests therein and for the development of the San Juan National Historical Park. 1 1 So in original. Probably should be “San Juan Island National Historical Park.”
