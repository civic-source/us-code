# § 6573. Restoration plans

- **(a)** Land enrolled in the healthy forests reserve program shall be subject to a restoration plan, to be developed jointly by the landowner and the Secretary of Agriculture, in coordination with the Secretary of 1 1 So in original. Probably should be “of the”. Interior.
- **(b)** The restoration plan shall require such restoration practices as are necessary to restore and enhance habitat for—
  - **(1)** species listed as endangered or threatened under section 1533 of this title ; and
  - **(2)** animal or plant species before the species reach threatened or endangered status, such as candidate, State-listed species, and special concern species.
