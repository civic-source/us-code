# § 6573. Restoration plans

- **(a)** Land enrolled in the healthy forests reserve program shall be subject to a restoration plan, to be developed jointly by the landowner and the Secretary of Agriculture, in coordination with the Secretary of the Interior.
- **(b)** The restoration plan shall require such restoration practices and measures as are necessary to restore and enhance habitat for species described in section 6572(b) of this title , including the following:
  - **(1)** Land management practices.
  - **(2)** Vegetative treatments.
  - **(3)** Structural practices and measures.
  - **(4)** Practices to increase carbon sequestration.
  - **(5)** Practices to improve biological diversity.
  - **(6)** Other practices and measures.
