# § 6578. Funding

- **(a)** Of the funds of the Commodity Credit Corporation, the Secretary of Agriculture shall make available $9,750,000 for each of fiscal years 2009 through 2012 to carry out this subchapter.
- **(b)** The funds made available under subsection (a) shall remain available until expended.
