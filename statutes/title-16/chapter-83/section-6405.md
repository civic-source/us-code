# § 6405. Emergency assistance

The Administrator may make grants to any State, local, or territorial government agency with jurisdiction over coral reefs for emergencies to address unforeseen or disaster-related circumstance 1 1 So in original. pertaining to coral reefs or coral reef ecosystems.
