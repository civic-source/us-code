# § 742m. Relinquishment of exclusive legislative jurisdiction

  - **(1)** by filing with the Governor (or, if none, the chief executive officer) of the State, Commonwealth, territory, or possession concerned, a notice of relinquishment to take effect upon acceptance thereof, or
  - **(2)** as the laws of the State, Commonwealth, territory, or possession may otherwise provide.
