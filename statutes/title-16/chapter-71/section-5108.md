# § 5108. Authorization of appropriations

- **(a)** To carry out this chapter, there are authorized to be appropriated $10,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2001 through 2005.
- **(b)** Amounts authorized under subsection (a) of this section may be used by the Secretary to support the Commission’s cooperative statistics program.
