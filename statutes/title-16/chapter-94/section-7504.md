# § 7504. Eligible uses

- **(a)** Amounts in the Fund may be allocated by the Foundation to support programs and activities intended to better understand and utilize ocean and coastal resources and coastal infrastructure, including baseline scientific research, ocean observing, and other programs and activities carried out in coordination with Federal and State departments or agencies.
- **(b)** No funds made available under this chapter may be used to—
  - **(1)** fund litigation against the Federal Government; or
  - **(2)** fund the creation of national marine monuments and marine protected areas, marine spatial planning, or the National Ocean Policy.
