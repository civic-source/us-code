# § 6211. Consistency with other law and rights

- **(a)** Nothing in this chapter limits or restricts any right, title, or interest of the United States in or to any land or resource.
- **(b)** In determining a cabin user fee in the State of Alaska, the Secretary shall not establish or impose a cabin user fee or a condition affecting a cabin user fee that is inconsistent with section 3193(d) of this title .
