# § 6210. Right of appeal and judicial review

- **(a)** Notwithstanding any action of a cabin owner to exercise rights in accordance with section 6209 of this title , the Secretary shall by regulation grant the cabin owner the right to an administrative appeal of the determination of a new base cabin user fee.
- **(b)** A cabin owner that is adversely affected by a final decision of the Secretary under this chapter may bring a civil action in United States district court.
