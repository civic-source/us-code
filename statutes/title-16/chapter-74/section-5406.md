# § 5406. Definitions

In this chapter:

  - **(1)** The term “Committee” means the Maritime Heritage Grants Advisory Committee established under section 5404 of this title .
  - **(2)** The term “National Trust” means the National Trust for Historic Preservation created by section 468 of this title .
  - **(3)** The term “private nonprofit organization” means any person that is exempt from taxation under section 501(a) of title 26 and described in section 501(c)(3) of title 26 .
  - **(4)** The term “Program” means the National Maritime Heritage Grants Program established by section 5403(a) of this title .
  - **(5)** The term “Secretary” means the Secretary of the Interior.
  - **(6)** The term “State Historic Preservation Officer” means a State Historic Preservation Officer appointed pursuant to paragraph (1)(A) of section 470a(b) of this title by the Governor of a State having a State Historic Preservation Program approved by the Secretary under that section.
