# § 6871. Definitions

In this chapter:

  - **(1)** The term “Federal fishing restriction” means a defined area in which all or certain fishing activities are temporarily or permanently prohibited or restricted by a Federal land or water management agency.
  - **(2)** The term “Federal land or water management agency” means—
    - **(A)** the Bureau of Reclamation;
    - **(B)** the National Park Service;
    - **(C)** the Bureau of Land Management;
    - **(D)** the United States Fish and Wildlife Service; and
    - **(E)** the Forest Service.
  - **(3)** The term “Federal waterway” means waters managed by 1 or more of the relevant Secretaries.
  - **(4)** The term “Federal waterway restriction” means a restriction on the access or use of a Federal waterway applied under applicable law by 1 or more of the Secretaries.
  - **(5)** The term “Secretaries” means—
    - **(A)** the Secretary of Agriculture, acting through the Chief of the Forest Service; and
    - **(B)** the Secretary of the Interior.
  - **(6)** The term “State” means each of the several States, the District of Columbia, and each territory of the United States.
