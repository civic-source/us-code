# § 972a. United States representation on the Council

- **(a)** The Secretary of State—
  - **(1)** shall appoint a United States representative to the Council; and
  - **(2)** may appoint not more than three alternate United States representatives to the Council.
- **(b)** An individual is not eligible for appointment as, or to serve as, the United States representative under subsection (a)(1) of this section unless the individual is an officer or employee of the United States Government.
- **(c)** An individual is not entitled to compensation for serving as the United States representative or an alternate United States representative.
- **(d)** While away from home or a regular place of business in the performance of service as the United States representative or an alternate United States representative, an individual is entitled to travel expenses, including per diem in lieu of subsistence, in the same manner as individuals employed intermittently in Government service are allowed expenses under section 5703(b) 1 1 See References in Text note below. of title 5.
