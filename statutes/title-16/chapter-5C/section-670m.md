# § 670m. Indian rights; State or Federal jurisdiction regulating Indian rights

  - **(1)** the rights of Indians or Indian tribes to the use of water or natural resources or their rights to fish, trap, or hunt wildlife as secured by statute, agreement, treaty, Executive order, or court decree; or
  - **(2)** existing State or Federal jurisdiction to regulate those rights either on or off reservations.
