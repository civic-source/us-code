# § 835l. Congressional declaration of financial policy; limitations on assistance; analyses and studies; “net revenues” defined

  - **(1)** all costs allocated to power at projects in the Pacific Northwest then existing or authorized, including the cost of acquiring power by purchase or exchange, and
  - **(2)** presently authorized assistance from power to irrigation at projects in the Pacific Northwest existing and authorized prior to September 7, 1966 .
