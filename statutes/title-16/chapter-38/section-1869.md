# § 1869. Restoration study

- **(a)** The Secretary may conduct a study to update scientific information and protocols needed to improve restoration techniques for a variety of coast habitat types and synthesize the results in a format easily understandable by restoration practitioners and local communities.
- **(b)** There are authorized to be appropriated $500,000 for fiscal year 2007 to conduct this study.
