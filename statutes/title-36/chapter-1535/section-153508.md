# § 153508. Principal office and location of activities and gardens

- **(a)** The principal office of the corporation shall be in the District of Columbia or another place decided by the board of trustees.
- **(b)** The activities of the corporation may be conducted anywhere. However, the corporation may establish tropical botanical gardens only in the United States and its territories and possessions.
