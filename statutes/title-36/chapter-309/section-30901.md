# § 30901. Organization

- **(a)** Boy Scouts of America (in this chapter, the “corporation”) is a body corporate and politic of the District of Columbia.
- **(b)** The domicile of the corporation is the District of Columbia.
- **(c)** Except as otherwise provided, the corporation has perpetual existence.
