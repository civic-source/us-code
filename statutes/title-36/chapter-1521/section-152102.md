# § 152102. Organization

- **(a)** National Mining Hall of Fame and Museum (in this chapter, the “corporation”), incorporated in Colorado, is a federally chartered corporation.
- **(b)** If the corporation does not comply with any provision of this chapter, the charter granted by this chapter expires.
