# § 22703. Membership

- **(a)** Except as provided in this chapter, eligibility for membership in the corporation and the rights and privileges of members are as provided in the constitution and bylaws of the corporation.
- **(b)** Each member has one vote in the conduct of official business at the post level.
- **(c)** The terms of membership may not discriminate on the basis of race, color, religion, sex, or national origin.
