# § 140404. Governing body

- **(a)** The composition of the board of directors of the corporation, and the responsibilities of the board, are as provided in the articles of incorporation and bylaws of the corporation.
- **(b)** The positions of officers of the corporation, and the election of the officers, are as provided in the articles of incorporation and bylaws.
