# § 70905. National officers

- **(a)** The national officers of the corporation are a student president, four student vice presidents (one from each of four regions of the United States established in the bylaws for purposes of administration of the corporation), a student secretary, an executive secretary, a treasurer, and a national advisor.
- **(b)** The national student officers of the corporation comprise a board of student officers. The board of student officers shall advise and make recommendations to the board of directors about the activities and business of the corporation.
- **(c)** The national officers of the corporation shall be elected annually by a majority vote of the delegates assembled in the annual national convention from among qualified members of the corporation, except that—
  - **(1)** the national advisor shall be the Secretary of Education;
  - **(2)** the executive secretary shall be a member of the Department of Education; and
  - **(3)** the treasurer shall be an employee or member of a State agency that directs or supervises a State program of agricultural education under the provisions of the Smith-Hughes Vocational Education Act ( 20 U.S.C. 11–15 , 16–28).
- **(d)** Each qualified delegate has one vote at the annual national convention.
