# § 110301. Organization

- **(a)** Jewish War Veterans, U.S.A., National Memorial, Incorporated (in this chapter, the “corporation”), is a federally chartered corporation.
- **(b)** The corporation is declared to be a nonprofit corporation incorporated and domiciled in the District of Columbia.
- **(c)** Except as otherwise provided, the corporation has perpetual existence.
