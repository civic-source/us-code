# § 21905. Restrictions

- **(a)** The corporation shall be nonprofit and may not issue stock.
- **(b)** The corporation shall be nonpolitical and nonsectarian, and may not promote the candidacy of an individual seeking public office.
- **(c)** The corporation may not have honorary members.
