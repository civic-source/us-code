# § 153701. Organization

- **(a)** National Woman’s Relief Corps, Auxiliary to the Grand Army of the Republic (in this chapter, the “corporation”), is a federally chartered corporation.
- **(b)** The corporation is declared to be incorporated and domiciled in the District of Columbia.
- **(c)** Except as otherwise provided, the corporation has perpetual existence.
