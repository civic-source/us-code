# § 220301. Organization

- **(a)** United States Capitol Historical Society (in this chapter, the “corporation”) is a federally chartered corporation.
- **(b)** The corporation is declared to be incorporated in the District of Columbia.
- **(c)** Except as otherwise provided, the corporation has perpetual existence.
