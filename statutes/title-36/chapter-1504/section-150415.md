# § 150415. Annual report

- **(a)** The corporation shall submit to Congress an annual report describing the activities of the corporation during the preceding fiscal year.
- **(b)** Each annual report under this section shall be submitted at the same time as the report of the audit of the corporation required by section 10101(b).
- **(c)** No annual report under this section shall be printed as a public document.
