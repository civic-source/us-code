# § 151701. Organization

- **(a)** National Film Preservation Foundation (in this chapter, the “corporation”) is a federally chartered corporation.
- **(b)** The corporation is a charitable and nonprofit corporation and is not an agency or establishment of the United States Government.
- **(c)** Except as otherwise provided, the corporation has perpetual existence.
