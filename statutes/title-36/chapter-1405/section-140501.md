# § 140501. Organization

- **(a)** Military Order of the Purple Heart of the United States of America, Incorporated (in this chapter, the “corporation”), is a federally chartered corporation.
- **(b)** The corporation is declared to be incorporated and domiciled in the District of Columbia.
- **(c)** Except as otherwise provided, the corporation has perpetual existence.
