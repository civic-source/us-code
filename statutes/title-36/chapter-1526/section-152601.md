# § 152601. Organization

- **(a)** The Help America Vote Foundation (in this chapter, the “foundation”) is a federally chartered corporation.
- **(b)** The foundation is a charitable and nonprofit corporation and is not an agency or establishment of the United States Government.
- **(c)** Except as otherwise provided, the foundation has perpetual existence.
