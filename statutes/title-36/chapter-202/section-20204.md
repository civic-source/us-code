# § 20204. Membership

- **(a)** Except as provided in this chapter, eligibility for membership in the corporation and the rights and privileges of members are as provided in the bylaws and articles of incorporation.
- **(b)** The terms of membership may not discriminate on the basis of race, color, religion, sex, disability, age, or national origin.
