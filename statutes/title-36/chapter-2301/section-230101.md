# § 230101. Organization

- **(a)** Veterans of Foreign Wars of the United States (in this chapter, the “corporation”), a national association of veterans who as soldiers, sailors, marines, and airmen served this Nation in wars, campaigns, and expeditions on foreign soil or in hostile waters, is a federally chartered corporation.
- **(b)** Except as otherwise provided, the corporation has perpetual existence.
