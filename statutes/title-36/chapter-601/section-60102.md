# § 60102. Organization

- **(a)** 82nd Airborne Division Association, Incorporated (in this chapter, the “corporation”), a nonprofit corporation incorporated in Illinois, is a federally chartered corporation.
- **(b)** If the corporation does not comply with any provision of this chapter, the charter granted by this chapter expires.
