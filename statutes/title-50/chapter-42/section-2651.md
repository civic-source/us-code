# § 2651. Prohibition on international inspections of Department of Energy facilities unless protection of Restricted Data is certified

- **(a)** The Secretary of Energy may not allow an inspection of a nuclear weapons facility by the International Atomic Energy Agency until the Secretary certifies to Congress that no Restricted Data will be revealed during such inspection.
