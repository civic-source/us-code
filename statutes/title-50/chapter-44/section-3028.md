# § 3028. General Counsel

- **(a)** There is a General Counsel of the Office of the Director of National Intelligence who shall be appointed by the President, by and with the advice and consent of the Senate.
- **(b)** The individual serving in the position of General Counsel may not, while so serving, also serve as the General Counsel of any other department, agency, or element of the United States Government.
- **(c)** The General Counsel is the chief legal officer of the Office of the Director of National Intelligence.
- **(d)** The General Counsel shall perform such functions as the Director of National Intelligence may prescribe.
