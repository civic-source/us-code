# § 3227c. Continued applicability of the Federal Advisory Committee Act to the Board of Visitors

The Federal Advisory Committee Act (5 U.S.C. App.) 1 1 See References in Text note below. shall continue to apply to the Board of Visitors of the National Intelligence University on and after the transfer date.
