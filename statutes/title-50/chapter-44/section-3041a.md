# § 3041a. Director of the National Reconnaissance Office

- **(a)** There is a Director of the National Reconnaissance Office.
- **(b)** The Director of the National Reconnaissance Office shall be appointed by the President, by and with the advice and consent of the Senate.
- **(c)** The Director of the National Reconnaissance Office shall be the head of the National Reconnaissance Office and shall discharge such functions and duties as are provided by this chapter or otherwise by law or executive order.
