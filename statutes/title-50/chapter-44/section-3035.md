# § 3035. Central Intelligence Agency

- **(a)** There is a Central Intelligence Agency.
- **(b)** The function of the Central Intelligence Agency is to assist the Director of the Central Intelligence Agency in carrying out the responsibilities specified in section 3036(c) of this title .
