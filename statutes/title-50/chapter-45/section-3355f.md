# § 3355f. Funding

- **(a)** There is hereby authorized to be appropriated to carry out the provisions of this subchapter amounts as follows:
  - **(1)** For fiscal year 2001, $650,000.
  - **(2)** For each fiscal year after fiscal year 2001, such sums as may be necessary for such fiscal year.
- **(b)** The President shall include in the budget submitted to Congress for each fiscal year under section 1105 of title 31 a request for amounts for the activities of the Board under this subchapter during such fiscal year.
