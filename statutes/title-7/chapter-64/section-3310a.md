# § 3310a. Research equipment grants

- **(a)** The Secretary may make competitive grants for the acquisition of special purpose scientific research equipment for use in the food and agricultural sciences programs of eligible institutions.
- **(b)** The amount of a grant made to an eligible institution under this section may not exceed $500,000.
- **(c)** The cost of acquisition or depreciation of equipment purchased with a grant under this section shall not be—
  - **(1)** charged as an indirect cost against another Federal grant; or
  - **(2)** included as part of the indirect cost pool for purposes of calculating the indirect cost rate of an eligible institution.
- **(d)** In this section, the term “eligible institution” means—
  - **(1)** a college or university; or
  - **(2)** a State cooperative institution.
- **(e)** There is authorized to be appropriated to carry out this section $5,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2019 through 2023.
