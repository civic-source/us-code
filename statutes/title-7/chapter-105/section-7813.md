# § 7813. Authorization of appropriations

- **(a)** There are authorized to be appropriated for each fiscal year such sums as are necessary to carry out this chapter.
- **(b)** Funds appropriated under subsection (a) of this section may not be used for the payment of the expenses or expenditures of the Board in administering a provision of an order.
