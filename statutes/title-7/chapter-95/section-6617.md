# § 6617. Authorization of appropriations and spending authority

- **(a)** Except as provided in subsection (b), there are authorized to be appropriated—
  - **(1)** an amount not to exceed 5 percent of the sum of—
    - **(A)** the sums received by the Secretary from sales of timber and other products of the forests; and
    - **(B)** user fees paid in connection with the use of forest lands; and
  - **(2)** such additional sums as may be necessary to carry out the purposes of this subchapter.
- **(b)** Subsection (a) shall not in any way affect payments to the States pursuant to section 500 of title 16 .
- **(c)** Any spending authority (as defined in section 651 of title 2 ) provided in this subchapter shall be effective for any fiscal year only to such extent or in such amounts as are provided in appropriation Acts.
