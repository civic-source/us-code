# § 6202. Definitions

As used in this chapter:

  - **(1)** The term “Board” means the Lime Board provided for under section 6204(b) of this title .
  - **(2)** The term “consumer information” means any action taken to provide information to, and broaden the understanding of, the general public regarding the use, nutritional attributes, and care of limes.
  - **(3)** The term “handle” means to sell, purchase, or package limes.
  - **(4)** The term “handler” means any person in the business of handling limes.
  - **(5)** The term “importer” means any person who imports limes into the United States.
  - **(6)** The term “lime” means the fruit of a citrus latifolia tree for the fresh market.
  - **(7)** The term “marketing” means the sale or other disposition of limes in commerce.
  - **(8)** The term “order” means a lime research, promotion, and consumer information order issued by the Secretary under section 6203(a) of this title .
  - **(9)** The term “person” means any individual, group of individuals, partnership, corporation, association, cooperative, or other legal entity.
  - **(10)** The term “producer” means any person who produces limes in the United States for sale in commerce.
  - **(11)** The term “producer-handler” means any person who is both a producer and handler of limes.
  - **(12)** The term “promotion” means any action taken under this chapter (including paid advertising) to present a favorable image for limes to the general public with the express intent of improving the competitive position and stimulating the sale of limes.
  - **(13)** The term “research” means any type of research relating to the use and nutritional value of limes and designed to advance the image, desirability, marketability, or quality of limes.
  - **(14)** The term “Secretary” means the Secretary of Agriculture.
  - **(15)** The term—
    - **(A)** “State” means each of the 50 States of the United States, the District of Columbia, and the Commonwealth of Puerto Rico; and
    - **(B)** “United States” means the 50 States of the United States, the District of Columbia, and the Commonwealth of Puerto Rico.
