# § 4813. Refunds

- **(a)** Notwithstanding any other provision of this chapter, prior to the approval of the continuation of an order pursuant to the referendum required under section 4811(a) of this title , any person shall have the right to demand and receive from the Board a refund of an assessment collected under section 4809 of this title if such person—
  - **(1)** is responsible for paying such assessment; and
  - **(2)** does not support the program established under this chapter.
- **(b)** Such demand shall be made in accordance with regulations, on a form, and within a time period prescribed by the Board and approved by the Secretary, but not later than 30 days after the end of the month in which the assessment was paid.

  - **(2)** did not collect such assessment from another producer, person, or importer.
