# § 2204j. National agriculture imagery program

- **(a)** The Secretary of Agriculture, acting through the Administrator of the Farm Service Agency, shall carry out a national agriculture imagery program to annually acquire aerial imagery during agricultural growing seasons from the continental United States.
- **(b)** The aerial imagery acquired under this section shall—
  - **(1)** consist of high resolution processed digital imagery;
  - **(2)** be made available in a format that can be provided to Federal, State, and private sector entities;
  - **(3)** be technologically compatible with geospatial information technology; and
  - **(4)** be consistent with the standards established by the Federal Geographic Data Committee.
- **(c)** The Secretary of Agriculture may supplement the aerial imagery collected under this section with satellite imagery.
- **(d)** There is authorized to be appropriated to carry out this section $23,000,000 for fiscal year 2019 and each fiscal year thereafter.
