# § 1471i. Administration

- **(a)** The Commodity Credit Corporation shall issue regulations to carry out this subchapter.
- **(b)** Such regulations shall establish procedures to ensure that the request for assistance by a Governor or county committee under section 1471b of this title , and individual applications of livestock producers under section 1471c of this title for assistance, are processed and decisions thereon are made as quickly as practicable.
- **(c)** For purposes of this subchapter, indigenous plants available to a livestock producer but not normally consumed by livestock as feed, such as cactus, may not be considered as feed on hand for such producers.
