# § 6902. Definitions

Except where the context requires otherwise, for purposes of this chapter:

  - **(1)** The term “Department” means the Department of Agriculture.
  - **(2)** The term “National Appeals Division” means the National Appeals Division of the Department established under section 6992 of this title .
  - **(3)** The term “Secretary” means the Secretary of Agriculture.
  - **(4)** The term “function” means an administrative, financial, or regulatory activity of an agency, office, officer, or employee of the Department.
