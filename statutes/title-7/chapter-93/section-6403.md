# § 6403. Authority to issue orders

- **(a)** To effectuate the declared policy under section 6401(b) of this title , the Secretary shall issue and from time to time may amend, orders applicable to all fluid milk processors, authorizing—
  - **(1)** the collection of assessments on fluid milk products subject to this chapter; and
  - **(2)** the use of the assessments to provide research and advertising in a manner prescribed by this chapter.
- **(b)** Any order issued under this chapter shall be national in scope.
- **(c)** Not more than one order shall be in effect under this chapter at any one time.
