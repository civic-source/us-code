# § 8201. Definitions

In this chapter:

  - **(1)** The term “eligible orchardist” means a person that produces annual crops from trees for commercial purposes.
  - **(2)** The term “natural disaster” means plant disease, insect infestation, drought, fire, freeze, flood, earthquake, lightning, and other occurrence, as determined by the Secretary.
  - **(3)** The term “Secretary” means the Secretary of Agriculture.
  - **(4)** The term “tree” includes a tree, bush, and vine.
