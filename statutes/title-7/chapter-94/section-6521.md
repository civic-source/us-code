# § 6521. Administration

- **(a)** Not later than 540 days after November 28, 1990 , the Secretary shall issue proposed regulations to carry out this chapter.
- **(b)**
  - **(1)** The Secretary shall provide technical, administrative, and National Institute of Food and Agriculture assistance to assist States in the implementation of an organic certification program under this chapter.
  - **(2)** The Secretary may provide financial assistance to any State that implements an organic certification program under this chapter.
