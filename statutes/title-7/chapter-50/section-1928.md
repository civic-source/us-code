# § 1928. Full faith and credit

- **(a)** A contract of insurance or guarantee executed by the Secretary under this chapter shall be an obligation supported by the full faith and credit of the United States.
- **(b)** A contract of insurance or guarantee executed by the Secretary under this chapter shall be incontestable except for fraud or misrepresentation that the lender or any holder—
  - **(1)** has actual knowledge of at the time the contract or guarantee is executed; or
  - **(2)** participates in or condones.
