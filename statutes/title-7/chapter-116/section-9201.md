# § 9201. Definitions

In this chapter:

  - **(1)** The term “animal” has the meaning given the term in section 8302 of this title .
  - **(2)** The term “transboundary disease” has the meaning given the term in section 8914(a) of this title .
  - **(3)** The term “veterinary countermeasure” has the meaning given the term in section 8302 of this title .
