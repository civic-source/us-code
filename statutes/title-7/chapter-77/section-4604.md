# § 4604. Notice and hearing

- **(a)** In issuing an order under this chapter, an amendment to an order, or a regulation to carry out this chapter, the Secretary shall comply with section 553 of title 5 .
- **(b)** Sections 556 and 557 of that title shall not apply with respect to the issuance of an order, an amendment to an order, or a regulation under this chapter.
- **(c)** A proposal for an order may be submitted to the Secretary by any organization or interested person affected by this chapter.
