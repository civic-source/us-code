# § 30303. State participation

- **(a)** A State may become a participating State under this chapter by notifying the Secretary of Transportation of its intention to be bound by section 30304 of this title .
- **(b)** A participating State may end its status as a participating State by notifying the Secretary of its withdrawal from participation in the National Driver Register.
- **(c)** Notification by a State under this section shall be made in the form and way the Secretary prescribes by regulation.
