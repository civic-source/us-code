# § 48103. Airport planning and development and noise compatibility planning and programs

- **(a)** There shall be available to the Secretary of Transportation out of the Airport and Airway Trust Fund established under section 9502 of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to make grants for airport planning and airport development under section 47104, airport noise compatibility planning under section 47505(a)(2), and carrying out noise compatibility programs under section 47504(c) $3,350,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2012 through 2015.
- **(b)** Amounts made available under subsection (a) shall remain available until expended.
