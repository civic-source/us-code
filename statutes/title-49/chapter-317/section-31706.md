# § 31706. Enforcement

- **(a)** On request of the Secretary of Transportation, the Attorney General may bring a civil action in a court of competent jurisdiction to enforce compliance with sections 31704 and 31705 of this title.
- **(b)** An action under this section may be brought only in the State in which an order is required to enforce compliance.
- **(c)** Subject to section 1341 of title 28 , the court, on a proper showing—
  - **(1)** shall issue a temporary restraining order or a preliminary or permanent injunction; and
  - **(2)** may require by the injunction that the State or any person comply with sections 31704 and 31705 of this title.
