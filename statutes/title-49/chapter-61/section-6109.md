# § 6109. Public education and awareness

- **(a)** The Secretary shall make a grant to an appropriate entity for promoting public education and awareness with respect to the 811 national excavation damage prevention phone number.
- **(b)** There is authorized to be appropriated to the Secretary $1,000,000 for the period beginning October 1, 2006 , and ending September 30, 2008 , to carry out this section.
