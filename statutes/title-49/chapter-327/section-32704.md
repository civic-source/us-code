# § 32704. Service, repair, and replacement

- **(a)** A person may service, repair, or replace an odometer of a motor vehicle if the mileage registered by the odometer remains the same as before the service, repair, or replacement. If the mileage cannot remain the same—
  - **(1)** the person shall adjust the odometer to read zero; and
  - **(2)** the owner of the vehicle or agent of the owner shall attach a written notice to the left door frame of the vehicle specifying the mileage before the service, repair, or replacement and the date of the service, repair, or replacement.
- **(b)** A person may not, with intent to defraud, remove or alter a notice attached to a motor vehicle as required by this section.
