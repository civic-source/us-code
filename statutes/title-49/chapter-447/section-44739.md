# § 44739. Pets on airplanes

- **(a)** It shall be unlawful for any person to place a live animal in an overhead storage compartment of an aircraft operated under part 121 of title 14, Code of Federal Regulations.
- **(b)** The Administrator may impose a civil penalty under section 46301 for each violation of this section.
