# § 233a. Permits or leases of certain lands in Okla­homa; retention of royalties

The Secretary of the Interior is directed to retain in his custody until otherwise directed by law the 12½ per centum and other royalties heretofore or hereafter received by him in pursuance of section 233 1 1 See References in Text note below. of this title.
