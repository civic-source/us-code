# § 1810. Authorization of appropriations

There are hereby authorized to be appropriated to carry out the provisions of this chapter a sum not to exceed $500,000 for the fiscal year ending September 30, 1985 , and such sums as may be necessary thereafter: Provided , That the authority provided for in this chapter shall expire on September 30, 1992 , unless otherwise authorized by Congress.
