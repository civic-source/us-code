# § 1009. Relinquishment of geothermal rights

  - **(1)** to make payment of all accrued rentals and royalties,
  - **(2)** to place all wells on the relinquished lands in condition for suspension or abandonment, and
  - **(3)** to protect or restore substantially the surface and surface resources.
