# § 101704. Reimbursable agreements

- **(a)** In carrying out work under reimbursable agreements with any State, local, or tribal government, the Secretary, without regard to any provision of law or a regulation—
  - **(1)** may record obligations against accounts receivable from those governments; and
  - **(2)** shall credit amounts received from those governments to the appropriate account.
- **(b)** Amounts shall be credited within 90 days of the date of the original request by the Service for payment.
