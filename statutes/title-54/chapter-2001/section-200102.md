# § 200102. Definitions

As used in this chapter:

  - **(1)** The term “State”, to the extent practicable, as determined by the Secretary, includes Puerto Rico, Guam, American Samoa, the Virgin Islands, and the Northern Mariana Islands.
  - **(2)** The term “United States”—
    - **(A)** includes the District of Columbia; and
    - **(B)** to the extent practicable, as determined by the Secretary, includes Puerto Rico, Guam, American Samoa, the Virgin Islands, and the Northern Mariana Islands.
