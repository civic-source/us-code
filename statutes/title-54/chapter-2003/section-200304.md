# § 200304. Statement of estimated requirements

- **(a)** There shall be submitted with the annual budget of the United States a comprehensive statement of estimated requirements during the ensuing fiscal year for appropriations from the Fund.
- **(b)** Of the total amount made available to the Fund through appropriations or deposited in the Fund under section 105(a)(2)(B) of the Gulf of Mexico Energy Security Act of 2006 ( 43 U.S.C. 1331 note; Public Law 109–432 )—
  - **(1)** not less than 40 percent shall be used for Federal purposes; and
  - **(2)** not less than 40 percent shall be used to provide financial assistance to States.
