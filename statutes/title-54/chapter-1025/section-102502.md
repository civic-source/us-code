# § 102502. Definition of museum object

In this chapter:

  - **(1)** The term “museum object” means an object that—
    - **(A)** typically is movable; and
    - **(B)** is eligible to be, or is made part of, a museum, library, or archive collection through a formal procedure, such as accessioning.
  - **(2)** The term “museum object” includes a prehistoric or historic artifact, work of art, book, document, photograph, or natural history specimen.
