# § 100102. Definitions

In this title:

  - **(1)** The term “Director” means the Director of the National Park Service.
  - **(2)** The term “National Park System” means the areas of land and water described in section 100501 of this title .
  - **(3)** The term “Secretary” means the Secretary of the Interior.
  - **(4)** The term “Service” means the National Park Service.
  - **(5)** The term “System” means the National Park System.
  - **(6)** The term “System unit” means one of the areas described in section 100501 of this title .
