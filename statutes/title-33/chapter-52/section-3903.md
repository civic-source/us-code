# § 3903. Applications

- **(a)** To receive assistance under this chapter, an eligible entity shall submit to the Secretary or the Administrator, as applicable, an application at such time, in such manner, and containing such information as the Secretary or the Administrator may require.
- **(b)** In the case of an eligible project described in paragraph (8) or (9) of section 3905 of this title , the Secretary or the Administrator, as applicable, shall require the eligible entity to submit a single application for the combined group of projects.
