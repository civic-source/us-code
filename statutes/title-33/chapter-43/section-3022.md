# § 3022. Personnel boards

- **(a)** At least once a year and at such other times as the Secretary determines necessary, the Secretary shall convene a personnel board. A personnel board shall consist of not less than five officers on the lineal list in the permanent grade of commander or above.
- **(b)** Each personnel board shall—
  - **(1)** recommend to the Secretary such changes in the lineal list as the board may determine; and
  - **(2)** make selections and recommendations to the Secretary and President for the appointment, promotion, separation, continuation, and retirement of officers as prescribed in this subchapter and subchapter III.
- **(c)** In a case in which any recommendation by a board convened under subsection (a) is not accepted by the Secretary or the President, the board shall make such further recommendations as are acceptable.
