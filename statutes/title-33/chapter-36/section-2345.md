# § 2345. Electronic submission of permit applications

- **(a)** Not later than 2 years after November 8, 2007 , the Secretary shall implement a program to allow electronic submission of permit applications for permits under the jurisdiction of the Secretary.
- **(b)** This section does not preclude the submission of a physical copy.
- **(c)** There is authorized to be appropriated to carry out this section $3,000,000.
