# § 3803. Administration and enforcement

- **(a)** Unless otherwise specified in this chapter, with respect to a vessel, the Secretary shall administer and enforce the Convention and this chapter.
- **(b)** Except with respect to section 3841(b) and (c) of this title, the Administrator shall administer and enforce subchapter III.
- **(c)** The Administrator and the Secretary may each prescribe and enforce regulations as may be necessary to carry out their respective responsibilities under this chapter.
