# § 4281. Strategy for improving post-consumer materials management and water management

- **(a)** Not later than 1 year after December 18, 2020 , the EPA Administrator shall, in consultation with stakeholders, develop a strategy to improve post-consumer materials management and infrastructure for the purpose of reducing plastic waste and other post-consumer materials in waterways and oceans.
- **(b)** On development of the strategy under subsection (a), the EPA Administrator shall—
  - **(1)** distribute the strategy to States; and
  - **(2)** make the strategy publicly available, including for use by—
    - **(A)** for-profit private entities involved in post-consumer materials management; and
    - **(B)** other nongovernmental entities.
