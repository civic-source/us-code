# § 535b. Presidential approval; recommendations of Federal officials

  - **(1)** the United States section of the International Boundary and Water Commission, United States and Mexico, in the case of a bridge connecting the United States and Mexico, and
  - **(2)** the heads of such departments and agencies of the Federal Government as he deems appropriate to determine the necessity for such bridge.
