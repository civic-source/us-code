# § 1971. Definitions

In this subchapter:

  - **(1)** The term “prize competition” means the competition for the award of the Genius Prize for Save Our Seas Innovations established under section 1972 of this title .
  - **(2)** The term “Secretary” means the Secretary of Commerce.
