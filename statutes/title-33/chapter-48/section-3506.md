# § 3506. Authorization of appropriations

- **(a)** In addition to the amounts authorized by section 892d of this title , there are authorized to be appropriated to the Administrator to carry out this chapter—
  - **(1)** $26,000,000 for fiscal year 2009;
  - **(2)** $32,000,000 for fiscal year 2010;
  - **(3)** $38,000,000 for fiscal year 2011; and
  - **(4)** $45,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2012 through 2015.
- **(b)** Of the amounts appropriated pursuant to subsection (a), the following amounts shall be used to carry out section 3504(c) of this title :
  - **(1)** $11,000,000 for fiscal year 2009.
  - **(2)** $12,000,000 for fiscal year 2010.
  - **(3)** $13,000,000 for fiscal year 2011.
  - **(4)** $15,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2012 through 2015.
- **(c)** To carry out interagency activities under section 3502 of this title , the head of any department or agency may execute a cooperative agreement with the Administrator, including those authorized by section 883e of this title .
