# § 3506. Authorization of appropriations

- **(a)** In addition to the amounts authorized by section 892d of this title , there are authorized to be appropriated to the Administrator to carry out this chapter $45,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2023 through 2028.
- **(b)** Of the amounts appropriated pursuant to subsection (a), the following amounts shall be used to carry out section 3504(c) 1 1 See References in Text Note below. of this title $15,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2023 through 2028.
- **(c)** Of amounts authorized pursuant to subsection (a), $20,000,000 is authorized to carry out section 3504a of this title .
