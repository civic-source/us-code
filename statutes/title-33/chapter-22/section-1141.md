# § 1141. Definitions

In this subchapter:

  - **(1)** The term “Sea Grant Institution” means a sea grant college or sea grant institute, as those terms are defined in section 1122 of this title .
  - **(2)** The term “Tribal organization” has the meaning given the term “tribal organization” in section 5304 of title 25 .
  - **(3)** The term “young fisherman” means an individual who—
    - **(A)** desires to participate in the commercial fisheries of the United States, including the Great Lakes fisheries;
    - **(B)** has worked as a captain, crew member, or deckhand on a commercial fishing vessel for not more than 10 years of cumulative service; or
    - **(C)** is a beginning commercial fisherman.
