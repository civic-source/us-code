# § 1400T. Special rules for mortgage revenue bonds

- **(a)** In the case of financing provided with respect to owner-occupied residences in the GO Zone, the Rita GO Zone, or the Wilma GO Zone, section 143 shall be applied—
  - **(1)** by treating any such residence in the Rita GO Zone or the Wilma GO Zone as a targeted area residence,
  - **(2)** by applying subsection (f)(3) thereof without regard to subparagraph (A) thereof, and
  - **(3)** by substituting “$150,000” for “$15,000” in subsection (k)(4) thereof.
- **(b)** Subsection (a) shall not apply to financing provided after December 31, 2010 .
