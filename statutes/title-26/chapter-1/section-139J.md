# § 139J. Certain contributions to Trump accounts

- **(a)** Gross income of an account beneficiary shall not include any qualified general contribution to a Trump account of the account beneficiary.
- **(b)** Any term used in this section which is used in section 530A shall have the meaning given such term under section 530A.
