# § 7326. Disposal of forfeited or abandoned property in special cases

- **(a)** Any coin-operated gaming device as defined in section 4462 1 1 See References in Text note below. upon which a tax is imposed by section 4461 1 and which has been forfeited under any provision of this title shall be destroyed, or otherwise disposed of, in such manner as may be prescribed by the Secretary.
- **(b)** For provisions relating to disposal of forfeited firearms, see section 5872(b).
