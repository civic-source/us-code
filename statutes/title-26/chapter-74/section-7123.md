# § 7123. Appeals dispute resolution procedures

- **(a)** The Secretary shall prescribe procedures by which any taxpayer may request early referral of 1 or more unresolved issues from the examination or collection division to the Internal Revenue Service Office of Appeals.
- **(b)**
  - **(1)** The Secretary shall prescribe procedures under which a taxpayer or the Internal Revenue Service Office of Appeals may request non-binding mediation on any issue unresolved at the conclusion of—
    - **(A)** appeals procedures; or
    - **(B)** unsuccessful attempts to enter into a closing agreement under section 7121 or a compromise under section 7122.
  - **(2)** The Secretary shall establish a pilot program under which a taxpayer and the Internal Revenue Service Office of Appeals may jointly request binding arbitration on any issue unresolved at the conclusion of—
    - **(A)** appeals procedures; or
    - **(B)** unsuccessful attempts to enter into a closing agreement under section 7121 or a compromise under section 7122.
