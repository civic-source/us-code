# § 2004. Administrative matters

- **(a)** The Secretary of the Interior shall provide all necessary administrative support to accomplish the requirements of sections 2002 and 2003 of this title.
- **(b)** There are authorized to be appropriated such sums as may be necessary to carry out the provisions of this chapter.
