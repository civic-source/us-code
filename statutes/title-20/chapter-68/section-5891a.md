# § 5891a. Definitions

In this Act:

  - **(1)** The terms “educational service agency”, “local educational agency”, “State educational agency”, and “outlying area” have the meanings given the terms in section 7801 of this title .
  - **(2)** The terms “eligible school attendance area” and “school attendance area” have the meanings given the terms in section 6313(a)(2) of this title .
  - **(3)** The term “Secretary” means the Secretary of Education.
  - **(4)** The term “State” means each of the 50 States, the District of Columbia, the Commonwealth of Puerto Rico, and each outlying area.
