# § 7267e. Administrative provisions

- **(a)** The Federal share of the cost described in section 7267b(b)(2) of this title shall be 50 percent.
- **(b)** The non-Federal share may be paid in cash or in kind (fairly evaluated, including plant, equipment, or services).
- **(c)** Not later than 2 years after the date funds are first made available to carry out this subpart, and every 2 years thereafter, the Secretary shall submit to the appropriate committees of Congress a report regarding activities assisted under this subpart.
