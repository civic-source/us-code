# § 7714. Authorization of appropriations

- **(a)** For the purpose of making payments under section 7702 of this title , there are authorized to be appropriated $32,000,000 for fiscal year 2000 and such sums as may be necessary for each of the seven succeeding fiscal years.
- **(b)** For the purpose of making payments under section 7703(b) of this title , there are authorized to be appropriated $809,400,000 for fiscal year 2000 and such sums as may be necessary for each of the seven succeeding fiscal years.
- **(c)** For the purpose of making payments under section 7703(d) of this title , there are authorized to be appropriated $50,000,000 for fiscal year 2000 and such sums as may be necessary for each of the seven succeeding fiscal years.
