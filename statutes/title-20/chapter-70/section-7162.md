# § 7162. Message and materials

- **(a)** Drug and violence prevention programs supported under this part shall convey a clear and consistent message that the illegal use of drugs and acts of violence are wrong and harmful.
- **(b)** The Secretary shall not prescribe the use of specific curricula for programs supported under this part.
