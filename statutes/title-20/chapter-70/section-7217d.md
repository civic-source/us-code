# § 7217d. Definitions

In this part:

  - **(1)** The term “local educational agency” means a local educational agency or a consortium of such agencies.
  - **(2)** The term “public school” means a public elementary school or a public secondary school.
  - **(3)** The term “school-age population” means the population aged 5 through 17.
  - **(4)** The term “State” means each of the 50 States, the District of Columbia, and the Commonwealth of Puerto Rico.
