# § 7122. Authorization of appropriations

- **(a)** There are authorized to be appropriated to carry out this subpart $1,650,000,000 for fiscal year 2017 and $1,600,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2018 through 2020.
- **(b)** Section 1223 of this title shall apply to this subpart.
