# § 6561c. State reservation

  - **(2)** or
  - **(3)** of section 6561a(a) of this title may reserve not more than 5 percent of the grant funds for administrative costs and State activities related to school dropout prevention and reentry activities, of which not more than 2 percent of the grant funds may be used for administrative costs.
