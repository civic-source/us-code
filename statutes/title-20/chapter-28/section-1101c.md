# § 1101c. Duration of grant

- **(a)** The Secretary may award a grant to a Hispanic-serving institution under this subchapter for 5 years.
- **(b)** Notwithstanding subsection (a) of this section, the Secretary may award a grant to a Hispanic-serving institution under this subchapter for a period of 1 year for the purpose of preparation of plans and applications for a grant under this subchapter.
