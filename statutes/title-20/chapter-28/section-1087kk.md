# § 1087kk. Amount of need

Except as otherwise provided therein, the amount of need of any student for financial assistance under this subchapter and part C of subchapter I of chapter 34 of title 42 (except subparts 1 1 So in original. Probably should be “subpart”. 1 or 2 of part A of this subchapter) is equal to—

  - **(1)** the cost of attendance of such student, minus
  - **(2)** the expected family contribution for such student, minus
  - **(3)** estimated financial assistance not received under this subchapter and part C of subchapter I of chapter 34 of title 42 (as defined in section 1087vv(j) of this title ).
