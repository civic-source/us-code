# § 4360a. Authorization of appropriations

- **(a)** There are authorized to be appropriated such sums as may be necessary for each of the fiscal years 2009 through 2014 to carry out the provisions of subchapter I and this subchapter, relating to—
  - **(1)** Gallaudet University;
  - **(2)** Kendall Demonstration Elementary School; and
  - **(3)** the Model Secondary School for the Deaf.
- **(b)** There are authorized to be appropriated such sums as may be necessary for each of the fiscal years 2009 through 2014 to carry out the provisions of subchapter I and this subchapter relating to the National Technical Institute for the Deaf.
