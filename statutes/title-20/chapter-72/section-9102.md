# § 9102. Institute of Museum and Library Services

- **(a)** There is established, within the National Foundation on the Arts and the Humanities, an Institute of Museum and Library Services.
- **(b)** The Institute shall consist of an Office of Museum Services and an Office of Library Services.
- **(c)** There shall be a National Museum and Library Services Board within the Institute, as provided under section 9105a of this title .
