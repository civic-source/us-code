# § 1111. Definitions

For purposes of this subchapter, the following terms apply:

  - **(1)** The term “appropriate congressional committees” means the Committee on Commerce, Science, and Transportation, the Committee on Banking, Housing, and Urban Affairs, and the Committee on Homeland Security and Governmental Affairs of the Senate and the Committee on Homeland Security and the Committee on Transportation and Infrastructure of the House of Representatives.
  - **(2)** The term “Department” means the Department of Homeland Security.
  - **(3)** The term “Secretary” means the Secretary of Homeland Security.
  - **(4)** The term “State” means any one of the 50 States, the District of Columbia, Puerto Rico, the Northern Mariana Islands, the Virgin Islands, Guam, American Samoa, and any other territory or possession of the United States.
  - **(5)** The term “terrorism” has the meaning that term has in section 101 of this title .
  - **(6)** The term “United States” means the 50 States, the District of Columbia, Puerto Rico, the Northern Mariana Islands, the Virgin Islands, Guam, American Samoa, and any other territory or possession of the United States.
