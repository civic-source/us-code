# § 925. Border Patrol unit for United States Virgin Islands

- **(a)** The Secretary may establish at least 1 Border Patrol unit for the United States Virgin Islands.
- **(b)** Not later than 180 days after October 13, 2006 , the Secretary shall submit a report to the appropriate congressional committees that includes the schedule, if any, for carrying out subsection (a).
