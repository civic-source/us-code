# § 295. Director of Shared Services

- **(a)** Within the Office of Deputy Secretary, there shall be a Director of Shared Services.
- **(b)** The Director of Shared Services shall be responsible for the coordination of resources for the Bureau of Border Security and the Bureau of Citizenship and Immigration Services, including—
  - **(1)** information resources management, including computer databases and information technology;
  - **(2)** records and file management; and
  - **(3)** forms management.
