# § 744. Establishment of national preparedness system

- **(a)** The President, acting through the Administrator, shall develop a national preparedness system to enable the Nation to meet the national preparedness goal.
- **(b)** The national preparedness system shall include the following components:
  - **(1)** Target capabilities and preparedness priorities.
  - **(2)** Equipment and training standards.
  - **(3)** Training and exercises.
  - **(4)** Comprehensive assessment system.
  - **(5)** Remedial action management program.
  - **(6)** Federal response capability inventory.
  - **(7)** Reporting requirements.
  - **(8)** Federal preparedness.
- **(c)** The national preparedness system may include national planning scenarios.
