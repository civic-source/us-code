# § 78o–4a. Commission Office of Municipal Securities

- **(a)** There shall be in the Commission an Office of Municipal Securities, which shall—
  - **(1)** administer the rules of the Commission with respect to the practices of municipal securities brokers and dealers, municipal securities advisors, municipal securities investors, and municipal securities issuers; and
  - **(2)** coordinate with the Municipal Securities Rulemaking Board for rulemaking and enforcement actions as required by law.
- **(b)** The head of the Office of Municipal Securities shall be the Director, who shall report to the Chairman.
- **(c)**
  - **(1)** The Office of Municipal Securities shall be staffed sufficiently to carry out the requirements of this section.
  - **(2)** The staff of the Office of Municipal Securities shall include individuals with knowledge of and expertise in municipal finance.
