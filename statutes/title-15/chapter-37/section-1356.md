# § 1356. Review and approval of plans and programs by Secretary

  - **(1)** bear the certification required by the Governor or his designee under section 1355 of this title ;
  - **(2)** comply with regulations and meet criteria that the Secretary shall promulgate and publish in the Federal Register; and
  - **(3)** otherwise accomplish the purpose of this chapter.
