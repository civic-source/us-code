# § 6555. Definitions

In this chapter:

  - **(1)** The term “Commission” means the Federal Trade Commission.
  - **(2)** The term “Internet” means collectively the myriad of computer and telecommunications facilities, including equipment and operating software, which comprise the interconnected world-wide network of networks that employ the Transmission Control Protocol/Internet Protocol, or any predecessor successor 1 1 So in original. Probably should be preceded by “or”. protocols to such protocol, to communicate information of all kinds by wire or radio.
