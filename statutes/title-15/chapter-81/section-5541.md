# § 5541. Definitions

In this subchapter:

  - **(1)** The term “Center” means a High-End Software Development Center established under section 5542(d) of this title .
  - **(2)** The term “high-end computing system” means a computing system with performance that substantially exceeds that of systems that are commonly available for advanced scientific and engineering applications.
  - **(3)** The term “Leadership System” means a high-end computing system that is among the most advanced in the world in terms of performance in solving scientific and engineering problems.
  - **(4)** The term “institution of higher education” has the meaning given the term in section 1001(a) of title 20 .
  - **(5)** The term “Secretary” means the Secretary of Energy, acting through the Director of the Office of Science of the Department of Energy.
