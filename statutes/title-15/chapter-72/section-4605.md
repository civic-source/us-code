# § 4605. Export of semiconductor manufacturing

Any export of materials, equipment, and technology developed by Sematech in whole or in part with financial assistance provided under section 4602(a) of this title shall be subject to the Export Administration Act of 1979 (50 U.S.C. App. 2401 et seq.) [now 50 U.S.C. 4601 et seq.] and shall not be subject to the Arms Export Control Act [ 22 U.S.C. 2751 et seq.].
