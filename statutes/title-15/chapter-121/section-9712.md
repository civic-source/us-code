# § 9712. Availability to the public of flood-related data

- **(a)** The Administrator shall make flood-related data available to the public on the website of the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration.
- **(b)** The Administrator may make the data under subsection (a) freely accessible or available at a cost that does not exceed the cost of preparing the data.
