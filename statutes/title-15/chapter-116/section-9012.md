# § 9012. Emergency rulemaking authority

Not later than 15 days after March 27, 2020 , the Administrator shall issue regulations to carry out this title 1 1 See References in Text note below. and the amendments made by this title 1 without regard to the notice requirements under section 553(b) of title 5 .
