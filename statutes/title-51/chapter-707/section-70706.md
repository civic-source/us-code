# § 70706. Public meetings, information, and hearings

- **(a)** A Commission shall—
  - **(1)** hold public hearings and meetings to the extent appropriate; and
  - **(2)** release public versions of the reports required under this chapter.
- **(b)** Any public hearings of a Commission shall be conducted in a manner consistent with the protection of information provided to or developed for or by the Commission as required by any applicable statute, regulation, or Executive order.
