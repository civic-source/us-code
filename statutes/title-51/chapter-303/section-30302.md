# § 30302. Quality assurance personnel

- **(a)** A person providing articles to the Administration under a contract entered into after December 9, 1991 , may not exclude Administration quality assurance personnel from work sites except as provided in a contract provision that has been submitted to Congress as provided in subsection (b).
- **(b)** The Administration shall not enter into any contract which permits the exclusion of Administration quality assurance personnel from work sites unless the Administrator has submitted a copy of the provision permitting such exclusion to Congress at least 60 days before entering into the contract.
