# § 51301. Definitions

In this chapter:

  - **(1)** The term “asteroid resource” means a space resource found on or within a single asteroid.
  - **(2)**
    - **(A)** The term “space resource” means an abiotic resource in situ in outer space.
    - **(B)** The term “space resource” includes water and minerals.
  - **(3)** The term “United States citizen” has the meaning given the term “citizen of the United States” in section 50902.
