# § 2077. Advances for enforcement of customs provisions

The Commissioner of Customs, 1 1 See Change of Name note below. with the approval of the Secretary of the Treasury, is authorized to direct the advance of funds by the Fiscal Service, Treasury Department, in connection with the enforcement of the customs laws.
