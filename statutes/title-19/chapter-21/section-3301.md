# § 3301. Definitions

For purposes of this Act:

  - **(1)** The term “Agreement” means the North American Free Trade Agreement approved by the Congress under section 3311(a) of this title .
  - **(2)** The term “HTS” means the Harmonized Tariff Schedule of the United States.
  - **(3)** Any reference to Mexico shall be considered to be a reference to the United Mexican States.
  - **(4)** Except as provided in section 3332 of this title , the term “NAFTA country” means—
    - **(A)** Canada for such time as the Agreement is in force with respect to, and the United States applies the Agreement to, Canada; and
    - **(B)** Mexico for such time as the Agreement is in force with respect to, and the United States applies the Agreement to, Mexico.
  - **(5)** The term “International Trade Commission” means the United States International Trade Commission.
  - **(6)** The term “Trade Representative” means the United States Trade Representative.
