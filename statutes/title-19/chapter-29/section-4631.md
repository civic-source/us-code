# § 4631. Definitions

In this subchapter:

  - **(1)** The term “labor attaché” means an individual hired under part B.
  - **(2)** The term “labor obligations” means the obligations under chapter 23 of the USMCA (relating to labor).
  - **(3)** The term “Mexico’s labor reform” means the legislation on labor reform enacted by Mexico on May 1, 2019 .
