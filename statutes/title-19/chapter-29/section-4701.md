# § 4701. Definitions

In this subchapter:

  - **(1)** The term “environmental law” has the meaning given the term in article 24.1 of the USMCA.
  - **(2)** The term “environmental obligations” means obligations relating to the environment under—
    - **(A)** chapter 1 of the USMCA (relating to initial provisions and general definitions); and
    - **(B)** chapter 24 of the USMCA (relating to environment).
