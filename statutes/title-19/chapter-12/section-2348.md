# § 2348. Protective provisions

- **(a)** Each recipient of adjustment assistance under this part shall keep records which fully disclose the amount and disposition by such recipient of the proceeds, if any, of such adjustment assistance, and which will facilitate an effective audit. The recipient shall also keep such other records as the Secretary may prescribe.
- **(b)** The Secretary and the Comptroller General of the United States shall have access for the purpose of audit and examination to any books, documents, papers, and records of the recipient pertaining to adjustment assistance under this part.
- **(c)** No adjustment assistance under this part shall be extended to any firm unless the owners, partners, or officers certify to the Secretary—
  - **(1)** the names of any attorneys, agents, and other persons engaged by or on behalf of the firm for the purpose of expediting applications for such adjustment assistance; and
  - **(2)** the fees paid or to be paid to any such person.
