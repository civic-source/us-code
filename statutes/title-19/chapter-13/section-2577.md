# § 2577. Definitions

Notwithstanding section 2571 of this title , for purposes of this part—

  - **(1)** The term “NAFTA” means the North American Free Trade Agreement.
  - **(2)** The term “State” means any of the several States, the District of Columbia, and the Commonwealth of Puerto Rico.
