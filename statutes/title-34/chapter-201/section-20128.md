# § 20128. Agency and department coordination

Each head of an Executive department (as defined in section 101 of title 5 ) responsible for carrying out a program under this Act, the Violence Against Women Act of 1994 (title IV of Public Law 103–322 ; 108 Stat. 1902 ), the Violence Against Women Act of 2000 (division B of Public Law 106–386 ; 114 Stat. 1491 ), the Violence Against Women and Department of Justice Reauthorization Act of 2005 (title IX of Public Law 109–162 ; 119 Stat. 3080 ) 1 1 See References in Text note below. , or the Violence Against Women Reauthorization Act of 2013 ( Public Law 113–4 ; 127 Stat. 54 ) may coordinate and collaborate on the prevention of domestic violence, dating violence, sexual assault, and stalking, including sharing best practices and efficient use of resources and technology for victims and those seeking assistance from the Federal Government.
