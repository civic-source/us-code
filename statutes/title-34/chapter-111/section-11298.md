# § 11298. Authority of Inspectors General

- **(a)** An Inspector General appointed under section 403 or 415 of title 5 may authorize staff to assist the National Center for Missing and Exploited Children—
  - **(1)** by conducting reviews of inactive case files to develop recommendations for further investigations; and
  - **(2)** by engaging in similar activities.
- **(b)**
  - **(1)** An Inspector General may not permit staff to engage in activities described in subsection (a) if such activities will interfere with the duties of the Inspector General under chapter 4 of title 5.
  - **(2)** No additional funds are authorized to be appropriated to carry out this section.
