# § 20306. Authorization of appropriations

- **(a)** There are authorized to be appropriated to carry out sections 20303 and 20304 of this title, $16,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2019 through 2023.
- **(b)** There are authorized to be appropriated to carry out section 20305 of this title , $5,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2019 through 2023.
