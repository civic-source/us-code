# § 2736d. Payne fellowship authorization

- **(a)** Undergraduate and graduate components of the Donald M. Payne International Development Fellowship Program may conduct outreach to attract outstanding students with an interest in pursuing a Foreign Service career who represent diverse ethnic and socioeconomic backgrounds.
- **(b)** The Secretary shall review past programs designed to increase minority representation in international affairs positions.
