# § 2736d–1. Donald M. Payne International Development Fellowship Program

(a) There shall be in the Department a Donald M. Payne International Development Fellowship Program.
(b) The program required by subsection (a) shall continue to provide financial assistance, mentoring, and professional development opportunities to individuals pursuing careers in international development.
(c) The program required by subsection (a) shall be administered by the Department, consistent with existing law and regulations.
