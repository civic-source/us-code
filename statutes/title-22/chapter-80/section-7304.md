# § 7304. Definitions

In this chapter:

  - **(1)** The term “DTS Network” means the worldwide telecommunications network supporting all United States Government agencies and departments operating from diplomatic and consular facilities outside of the United States.
  - **(2)** The term “DTS–PO” means the Diplomatic Telecommunications Service Program Office.
  - **(3)** The term “Governance Board” means the Diplomatic Telecommunications Service Governance Board established under section 7302(a)(1) of this title .
