# § 2349aa–7. Coordination of all United States terrorism-related assistance to foreign countries

- **(a)** The Secretary of State shall be responsible for coordinating all assistance related to international terrorism which is provided by the United States Government to foreign countries.
- **(b)**
- **(c)** Nothing contained in this section shall be construed to limit or impair the authority or responsibility of any other Federal agency with respect to law enforcement, domestic security operations, or intelligence activities as defined in Executive Order 12333.
