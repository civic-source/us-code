# § 6801. Definitions

In this chapter:

  - **(1)** The term “AIDS” means the acquired immune deficiency syndrome.
  - **(2)** The term “Association” means the International Development Association.
  - **(3)** The term “Bank” or “World Bank” means the International Bank for Reconstruction and Development.
  - **(4)** The term “HIV” means the human immunodeficiency virus, the pathogen which causes AIDS.
  - **(5)** The term “HIV/AIDS” means, with respect to an individual, an individual who is infected with HIV or living with AIDS.
