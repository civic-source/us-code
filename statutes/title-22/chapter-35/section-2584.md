# § 2584. Dual compensation exemption

Members of advisory boards and consultants may serve as such without regard to any Federal law limiting the reemployment of retired officers or employees or governing the simultaneous receipt of compensation and retired pay or annuities, subject to section 5532 1 1 See Codification note below. of title 5. This section shall apply only to individuals carrying out activities related to arms control, nonproliferation, and disarmament.
