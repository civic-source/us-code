# § 9401. Definitions

In this chapter:

  - **(1)** The term “act of international terrorism” has the meaning given that term in section 14 of the Iran Sanctions Act of 1996 ( Public Law 104–172 ; 50 U.S.C. 1701 note).
  - **(2)** The term “appropriate congressional committees” has the meaning given that term in section 14 of the Iran Sanctions Act of 1996 ( Public Law 104–172 ; 50 U.S.C. 1701 note).
  - **(3)** The term “foreign person” means a person that is not a United States person.
  - **(4)** The term “Iranian person” means—
    - **(A)** an individual who is a citizen or national of Iran; or
    - **(B)** an entity organized under the laws of Iran or otherwise subject to the jurisdiction of the Government of Iran.
  - **(5)** The term “IRGC” means Iran’s Islamic Revolutionary Guard Corps.
  - **(6)** The term “knowingly” has the meaning given that term in section 14 of the Iran Sanctions Act of 1996 ( Public Law 104–172 ; 50 U.S.C. 1701 note).
  - **(7)** The term “United States person” means—
    - **(A)** a United States citizen or an alien lawfully admitted for permanent residence to the United States; or
    - **(B)** an entity organized under the laws of the United States or of any jurisdiction within the United States, including a foreign branch of such an entity.
