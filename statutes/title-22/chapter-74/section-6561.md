# § 6561. Abolition of United States International Development Cooperation Agency

- **(a)** Except for the components specified in subsection (b) of this section, the United States International Development Cooperation Agency (including the Institute for Scientific and Technological Cooperation) is abolished.
- **(b)** Subsection (a) of this section does not apply to the Agency for International Development or the Overseas Private Investment Corporation.
