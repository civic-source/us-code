# § 9681. Definitions

In this subchapter:

  - **(1)** The term “agency” includes any entity, organizational unit, program, or function.
  - **(2)** The term “transition period” means the period—
    - **(A)** beginning on October 5, 2018 ; and
    - **(B)** ending on the effective date of the reorganization plan required by section 9682(e) of this title .
