# § 1642h. Prohibition against payment of award to certain persons

  - **(1)** any person who has been convicted of a violation of any provision of chapter 115 of title 18, or of any other crime involving disloyalty to the United States, or
  - **(2)** any claimant whose claim under this subchapter is within the scope of subchapter III of this chapter.
