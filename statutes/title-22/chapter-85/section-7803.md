# § 7803. Definitions

In this chapter:

  - **(1)** The term “appropriate congressional committees” means—
    - **(A)** the Committee on Foreign Affairs of the House of Representatives; and
    - **(B)** the Committee on Foreign Relations of the Senate.
  - **(2)** The term “China” means the People’s Republic of China.
  - **(3)** The term “humanitarian assistance” means assistance to meet humanitarian needs, including needs for food, medicine, medical supplies, clothing, and shelter.
  - **(4)** The term “North Korea” means the Democratic People’s Republic of Korea.
  - **(5)** The term “North Koreans” means persons who are citizens or nationals of North Korea.
  - **(6)** The term “South Korea” means the Republic of Korea.
