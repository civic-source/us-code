# § 3304. Overseas Private Investment Corporation

- **(a)** During the three-year period beginning on April 10, 1979 , the $1,000 per capita income restriction in clause (2) of the second undesignated paragraph of section 2191 of this title shall not restrict the activities of the Overseas Private Investment Corporation in determining whether to provide any insurance, reinsurance, loans, or guaranties with respect to investment projects on Taiwan.
- **(b)** Except as provided in subsection (a) of this section, in issuing insurance, reinsurance, loans, or guaranties with respect to investment proj­ects on Taiwan, the Overseas Private Insurance 1 1 So in original.
