# § 3015. Nonmailable plant pests and injurious animals

- **(a)** Any injurious animal, the importation or interstate shipment of which is prohibited pursuant to section 42 of title 18 , constitutes nonmailable matter.
- **(b)** Any plant pest, the movement of which is prohibited pursuant to section 103 or 104 of the Federal Plant Pest Act ( 7 U.S.C. 150bb or 150cc), 1 1 See References in Text note below. constitutes nonmailable matter.
- **(c)** Any plant, article, or matter, the importation or interstate shipment of which is prohibited pursuant to the Act of August 20, 1912 ( 37 Stat. 315 , chapter 308; 7 U.S.C. 151 et seq.) (commonly known as the “Plant Quarantine Act”), 1 constitutes nonmailable matter.
- **(d)** Any fish, wildlife, or plant, the conveyance of which is prohibited pursuant to section 3 of the Lacey Act Amendments of 1981 ( 16 U.S.C. 3372 ), constitutes nonmailable matter.
