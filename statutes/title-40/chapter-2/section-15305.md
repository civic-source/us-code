# § 15305. Meetings

- **(a)** Each Commission shall hold an initial meeting not later than 180 days after the date of the enactment of this section.
- **(b)** Each Commission shall conduct at least 1 meeting each year with the Federal Cochairperson and at least a majority of the State members present.
- **(c)** Each Commission shall conduct additional meetings at such times as it determines and may conduct such meetings by electronic means.
