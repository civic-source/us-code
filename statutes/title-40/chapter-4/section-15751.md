# § 15751. Authorization of appropriations

- **(a)** There is authorized to be appropriated to each Commission to carry out this subtitle $30,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2008 through 2012.
- **(b)** Not more than 10 percent of the funds made available to a Commission in a fiscal year under this section may be used for administrative expenses.
