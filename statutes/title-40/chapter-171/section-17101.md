# § 17101. Definitions

In this chapter, the following definitions apply:

  - **(1)** The term “Federal Government” includes the government of the District of Columbia.
  - **(2)** The term “motor vehicle” means a vehicle, self-propelled or drawn by mechanical power, designed for use on the highways principally for the transportation of passengers, except a vehicle designed or used for military field training, combat, or tactical purposes.
