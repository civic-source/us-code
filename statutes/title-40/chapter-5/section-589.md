# § 589. Installation, repair, and replacement of sidewalks

- **(a)** An executive agency may install, repair, and replace sidewalks around buildings, installations, property, or grounds that are—
  - **(1)** under the agency’s control;
  - **(2)** owned by the Federal Government; and
  - **(3)** located in a State, the District of Columbia, Puerto Rico, or a territory or possession of the United States.
- **(b)** Subsection (a) may be carried out by—
  - **(1)** reimbursement to a State or political subdivision of a State, the District of Columbia, Puerto Rico, or a territory or possession of the United States; or
  - **(2)** a means other than reimbursement.
- **(c)** Subsection (a) shall be carried out in accordance with regulations the Administrator of General Services prescribes with the approval of the Director of the Office of Management and Budget.
- **(d)** Amounts appropriated to an executive agency for installation, repair, and maintenance, generally, are available to carry out this section.
- **(e)** This section does not increase or enlarge the tort liability of the Government for injuries to individuals or damages to property.
