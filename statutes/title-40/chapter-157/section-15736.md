# § 15736. Southern New England Regional Commission

The region of the Southern New England Regional Commission shall include the following counties:

  - **(1)** Each county in the State of Rhode Island.
  - **(2)** The counties of Hartford, Middlesex, New Haven, New London, Tolland, and Windham in the State of Connecticut.
  - **(3)** Each county in the Commonwealth of Massachusetts.
