# § 942–7. Military park, Indian or other reservation

Sections 687a, 687a–2 to 687a–5, 1 1 See References in Text note below. and 942–1 to 942–9 of this title and sections 607a and 615a of title 16 shall not apply to any lands within the limits of any military park, Indian, or other reservation unless such right of way shall be provided for by Act of Congress.
