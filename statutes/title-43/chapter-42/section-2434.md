# § 2434. Termination of authority

- **(a)** Subject to subsection (b), the authority of the Secretary to carry out this subchapter terminates on the date that is 10 years after December 22, 2006 .
- **(b)** The termination of authority under subsection (a) shall have no effect on—
  - **(1)** any loans guaranteed by the United States under this subchapter; or
  - **(2)** the administration of any loan guaranteed under this subchapter before the effective date of the termination of authority.
