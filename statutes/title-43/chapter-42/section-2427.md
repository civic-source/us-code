# § 2427. Water rights

- **(a)** Nothing in this subchapter preempts or affects State water law or an interstate compact governing water.
- **(b)** The Secretary shall comply with State water laws in carrying out this subchapter. Nothing in this subchapter affects or preempts State water law or an interstate compact governing water.
