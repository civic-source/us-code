# § 390h–39. City of Corona Water Utility, California, water recycling and reuse project

- **(a)** The Secretary, in cooperation with the City of Corona Water Utility, California, is authorized to participate in the design, planning, and construction of, and land acquisition for, a project to reclaim and reuse wastewater, including degraded groundwaters, within and outside of the service area of the City of Corona Water Utility, California.
- **(b)** The Federal share of the cost of the project authorized by this section shall not exceed 25 percent of the total cost of the project.
- **(c)** The Secretary shall not provide funds for the operation and maintenance of the project authorized by this section.
