# § 390h–29. Redwood City recycled water project

- **(a)** The Secretary, in cooperation with the City of Redwood City, California, is authorized to participate in the design, planning, and construction of recycled water system facilities.
- **(b)** The Federal share of the cost of the project authorized by this section shall not exceed 25 percent of the total cost of the project.
- **(c)** The Secretary shall not provide funds for the operation and maintenance of the project authorized by this section.
- **(d)** There is authorized to be appropriated to carry out this section $1,100,000.
