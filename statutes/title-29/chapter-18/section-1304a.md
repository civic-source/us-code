# § 1304a. Sponsor education and assistance

- **(a)** In this section, the term “CSEC plan” has the meaning given that term in subsection (f)(1) of section 1060 of this title .
- **(b)** The Participant and Plan Sponsor Advocate established under section 1304 of this title shall make itself available to assist CSEC plan sponsors and participants as part of the duties it performs under the general supervision of the Board of Directors under section 1304(b) of this title .
