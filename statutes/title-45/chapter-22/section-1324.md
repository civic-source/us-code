# § 1324. Certain enforcement relief

- **(a)** The Secretary of Transportation, with respect to any provision of section 1321 or 1322 of this title, and any person who suffers direct and substantial economic injury as a result of an alleged violation by the Corporation, with respect to the provisions of section 1321(a)(1) and (2) 1 1 See References in Text note below. of this title, and section 1322 of this title , may bring an action to require compliance with such provision.
- **(b)** Any action brought under this subchapter shall be brought before the special court established under section 719 of this title . Such special court may limit the enforcement of a restriction under section 1321 of this title , if the effect of such restriction would be to substantially impair the continued viability of the Corporation.
