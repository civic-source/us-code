# § 4820. National technology and industrial base plans, policy, and guidance

- **(a)** The Secretary of Defense shall prescribe regulations requiring consideration of the national technology and industrial base, in accordance with the strategy required by section 4811 of this title , in the development and implementation of acquisition plans for each major defense acquisition program.
- **(b)** The Secretary of Defense shall develop and promulgate acquisition policy and guidance to the service acquisition executives, the heads of the appropriate Defense Agencies and Department of Defense Field Activities, and relevant program managers.
