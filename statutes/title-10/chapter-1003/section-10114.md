# § 10114. Coast Guard Reserve

As provided in section 701 1 1 See References in Text note below. of title 14, the Coast Guard Reserve is a component of the Coast Guard and is organized, administered, trained, and supplied under the direction of the Commandant of the Coast Guard. Laws applicable to the Coast Guard Reserve are set forth in chapter 21 1 of title 14 ( 14 U.S.C. 701 et seq.).
