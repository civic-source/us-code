# § 20602. Officers: computation of years of service for voluntary retirement

- **(a)** For the purpose of determining whether an officer of the Space Force may be retired under section 20601 of this title , the officer’s years of service are computed by adding all active service in the armed forces.
- **(b)** Section 972(b) of this title excludes from computation of an officer’s years of service for purposes of this section any time identified with respect to that officer under that section.
