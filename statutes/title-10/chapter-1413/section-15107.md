# § 15107. Retirement: retirement for years of service; selective early retirement

- **(a)** Sections 14507 through 14515 of this title shall apply to the retirement of officers in competitive categories of officers designated for purposes of this chapter.
- **(b)** Section 14101(b) of this title shall apply to the retirement of officers described in subsection (a).
