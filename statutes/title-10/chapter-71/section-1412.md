# § 1412. Administrative provisions

- **(a)** Amounts computed under this chapter, if not a multiple of $1, shall be rounded to the next lower multiple of $1.
- **(b)** Amounts of retired pay and retainer pay due a retired member of the uniformed services shall be paid on the first day of each month beginning after the month in which the right to such pay accrues.
