# § 9089. Program Executive Officer for Assured Access to Space

- **(a)**
  - **(1)** There is a Program Executive Officer for Assured Access to Space within the Space Force.
  - **(2)** The Program Executive Officer for Assured Access to Space shall serve a term of four years.
- **(b)** The Program Executive Officer for Assured Access to Space, while so serving, shall have the grade of brigadier general, major general, lieutenant general, or general.
- **(c)** The Program Executive Officer for Assured Access to Space, while serving as the Program Executive Officer, shall also serve as the Commander of Space Launch Delta 45.
