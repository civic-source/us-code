# § 3535. Multiyear contracts for acquisition of services: notice to congressional committees before taking certain actions

- **(a)** The head of an agency may not initiate under this subchapter a contract for services that includes an unfunded contingent liability in excess of $20,000,000 unless the congressional defense committees are notified of the proposed contract at least 30 days in advance of the award of the proposed contract.
- **(b)** The head of an agency may not terminate a multiyear procurement contract for services until 10 days after the date on which notice of the proposed termination is provided to the congressional defense committees.
