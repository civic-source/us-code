# § 437. Congressional oversight

- **(a)** Copies of regulations proposed to be prescribed under section 436 of this title (including any proposed revision to such regulations) shall be submitted to the intelligence committees not less than 30 days before they take effect.
- **(b)** Consistent with title V of the National Security Act of 1947 ( 50 U.S.C. 413 et seq.), 1 1 See References in Text note below. the Secretary of Defense shall ensure that the intelligence committees are kept fully and currently informed of actions taken pursuant to this subchapter, including any significant anticipated activity to be authorized pursuant to this subchapter.
