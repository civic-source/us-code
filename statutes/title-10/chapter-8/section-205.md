# § 205. Missile Defense Agency

- **(a)** The Director of the Missile Defense Agency shall be appointed for a six-year term.
- **(b)** The Missile Defense Agency shall be under the authority, direction, and control of the Under Secretary of Defense for Research and Engineering.
