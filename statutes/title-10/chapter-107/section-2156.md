# § 2156. Joint Forces Staff College: duration of principal course of instruction

- **(a)** The duration of the principal course of instruction offered at the Joint Forces Staff College may not be less than 10 weeks of resident instruction.
- **(b)** In this section, the term “principal course of instruction” means any course of instruction offered at the Joint Forces Staff College as Phase II joint professional military education.
