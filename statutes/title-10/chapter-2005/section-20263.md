# § 20263. Entitlement of officers discharged or retired under this chapter to separation pay or retired pay

- **(a)** An officer who is discharged under this chapter is entitled, if eligible therefor, to separation pay under section 1174 of this title .
- **(b)** An officer who is retired under this chapter is entitled to retired pay computed under chapter 71 or 1223 of this title, as applicable.
