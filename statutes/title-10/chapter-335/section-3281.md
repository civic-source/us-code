# § 3281. Commissioned officer grades

The commissioned grades in the Regular Army are:

  - **(1)** Major general.
  - **(2)** Brigadier general.
  - **(3)** Colonel.
  - **(4)** Lieutenant colonel.
  - **(5)** Major.
  - **(6)** Captain.
  - **(7)** First lieutenant.
  - **(8)** Second lieutenant.
