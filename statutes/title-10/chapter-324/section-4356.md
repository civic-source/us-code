# § 4356. Selected acquisition reports: time for submission to Congress; form of report

- **(a)** Each comprehensive annual Selected Acquisition Report shall be submitted within 30 days after the date on which the President transmits the Budget to Congress for the following fiscal year, and each Quarterly Selected Acquisition Report shall be submitted within 45 days after the end of the fiscal-year quarter.
- **(b)** A Selected Acquisition Report required under this chapter shall be submitted in unclassified form without any designation relating to dissemination control, but may contain a classified annex.
