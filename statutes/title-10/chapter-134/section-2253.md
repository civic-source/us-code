# § 2253. Motor vehicles

- **(a)** The Secretary of Defense and the Secretary of each military department may—
  - **(1)** provide for insurance of official motor vehicles in a foreign country when the laws of such country require such insurance; and
  - **(2)** purchase right-hand drive passenger sedans at a cost of not more than $30,000 each.
- **(b)** Amounts appropriated to the Department of Defense for operation and maintenance of the active forces may be used for the hire of passenger motor vehicles.
