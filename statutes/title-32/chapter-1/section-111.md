# § 111. Suspension of certain provisions of this title

In time of war, or of emergency declared by Congress, the President may suspend the operation of any provision of sections 307(e), 309, 310, and 323(d) and (e) 1 1 See References in Text note below. of this title with respect to the Army National Guard or the Air National Guard.
