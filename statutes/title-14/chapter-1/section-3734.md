# § 3734. Effect of this subchapter on retirement and retired pay

Except as provided in subsection 3757(b) 1 1 So in original. Probably should be “section 3757(b)”. of this title, nothing in this subchapter authorizes the retirement of a Reserve officer or the payment of retired, retainer, or severance pay to a Reserve officer; or affects in any manner the law relating to the retirement of, or the granting of retired or retainer pay or other benefits to a Reserve officer.
